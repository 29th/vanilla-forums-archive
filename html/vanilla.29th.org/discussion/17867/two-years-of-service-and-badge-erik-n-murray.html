Two Years of Service and Badge (Erik N. Murray)

T5 Vonk [Ret.]
April 2017 edited April 2017 in Promotions and Awards

WO1 Kear said:

Sgt. Murray stands before you all at an important milestone in his 29th career. The Sergeant is no stranger to us in CP2, he is known for his firm, but fair, battlefield demeanour and is often seen at the helm of squad one: spearheading any assault no matter how dangerous or fraught - he simply nods, plans, and gets the job done against all odds.

Not only has the Sergeant shot, shouted and stormed his way through these two years of service, but he has also done so with diligence and pride, CP2S1 recently received the Superior Unit Citation and this is, in part, testament to Sgt. Murray's tenacious leadership of the squad, which has excelled under his watchful eye.

Here's to a fantastic squad leader, exemplary NCO and a very Norwegian pillar of CP2! Congratulations on your fourth army of occupation medal and first world war one victory medal for two years of service!


Cpl. van Gastel said:

This Norwegian old fart from S1 has been here, destroying the enemy forces with his M4. But he's not here to brag about his rifle skills. This man has done quite amazing work on providing effective and selective covering fire with this machine gun, cutting off heads and slicing trough chests with his M249. I am honored to stand beside the Sergeant, seeing him being awarded his well-deserved Marksman Arms Qualification Badge for the Automatic Rifle. Congratulations, Sgt. Murray!
