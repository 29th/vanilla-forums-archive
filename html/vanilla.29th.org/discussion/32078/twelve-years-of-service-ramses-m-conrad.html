Twelve Years of Service (Ramses M. Conrad)

CW4 Slomczynski
June 2019 in Promotions and Awards

Lt. Col. Wheatley said:

We often say that the 29th is bigger than any one man, but if MSgt. Conrad were not in the 29th, the experience we all enjoy would not be the same. Whether it's stepping in to help Lighthouse cover BCT to helping companies develop their NCOs to reorganizing policies and procedures to ensure clarity across the Battalion, MSgt. sure has his hands dirty. It's funny to think back to the DoD days and how we all told ourselves we were only going to be in the 29th for a few months, a year tops, and yet, here's MSgt. Conrad getting his TWENTY FOURTH Army of Occupation Medal and SIXTH WWI Victory Medal for TWELVE years of dedicated service to this unit. I'm thankful for your twelve years of service, MSgt. Conrad, I know the rest of the Battalion is as well!
*Salute*
