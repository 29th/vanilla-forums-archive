Promotion (Jack C. Brand)

Cpt. Hill [Ret.]
January 2015 edited January 2015 in Promotions and Awards
FSgt. Hill wrote:
PFC Brand has always been a dedicated member of Charlie Company.  From his first days until today I have witnessed his growth within the 29th.  I am proud to say he has made leaps and bounds and I am confident he will contiune to show us all what it means to provide dedicated service and continued friendship.  More recently PFC Brand has taken on the role of DI for Arma 3 and has become a shining example of what that role means to the 29th.  Perservering through a brand new BCT program for a game that is still new to most he has shown exeplary leadership qualities and I am honoured to reward him for his efforts both on and off the battlefield by promoting him to Corporal.
