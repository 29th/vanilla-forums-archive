Eight Years of Service (Chris R. Cyr)

T3 Lauritsen
November 2016 edited November 2016 in Promotions and Awards

FSgt. at day, librarian at night. When he is not busy yelling at teenagers at drills he is, you guessed it, still yelling at teenagers, but at the local library. I know that I am not the first to state this, but FSgt. Cyr is a true inspiration and you can ask nothing more from a member of the 29th. Not only do I get to work along with him in Able Company, but he also assists me in the Lighthouse office, and believe me when I say, things would be a lot more difficult, was it not for him. So for FSgt. Cyr's 8 years of faitful service, I am happy to be awarding him his 16th Army of Occupation medal along with his 4th World War I Victory Medal.

Stay cool FSgt!
