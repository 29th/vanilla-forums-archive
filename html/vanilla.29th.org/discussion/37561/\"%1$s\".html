Award (Fabian G. Yukki)

SSgt. Novak
May 2020 edited May 2020 in Promotions and Awards

SSgt. Novak said:

During the GMT Dog Company drills held on 26th of April, 2020, T/5 Yukki was among the members of DP1 which was involved in harsh close combat among the alleys and buildings of Saigon map heading towards that round's objective. Suffering heavy casualties from constantly counter-attacking enemy which was in a possession of huge Cathedral using it as a cover, the progress of DP1 was halted, but that’s where and when T/5 Yukki’s well known SMG skill performed at its best. He dashed towards the enemy lines, almost instantly putting down 3 enemies (easing the pressure on nearby forces and allowing them to continue their advance) and, despite the spray of bullets still coming from the urban quarters behind and being virtually on his own for a second, rushed through the maze of alleys towards the objective itself fighting off the remaining enemies en route. Leaving nothing short of the path of total destruction behind him, he reached the enclosed square (the objective itself) and, already having 7 kills on his list, faced the uneasy situation having few more enemies in the possession of the objective and not having enough time to capture it even with a bunch of friendly forces running to his aid but still being way behind him. With zero hesitation, he alone entered the objective’s premises and took the only possible correct decision available at that moment – to kill the remaining 3 enemies and make it a victory by team elimination, topping his kill score to 10 people, which was approximately 40 % of all enemy troops fielded for that round. Due to his outstanding actions which converted the near defeat to the complete victory and as an example of his skill, determination and unwillingness to surrender in a face of superior enemy, I award him Bronze Star! Congratulations, T/5!
