Award (Lars Burg)

Cpt. Hill [Ret.]
January 2018 edited January 2018 in Promotions and Awards

T4 Burg has been Charlie's Company clerk for approaching one year now and has never faltered in his duties, in fact, he has gone above and beyond with initiatives of his own that were useful for Charlie's overall goals. Furthermore, the T/4 has always been there to oversee the Platoon clerks in training and quality control. He also occupies 8 staff jobs which he performs to a high standard. Prior to his assignment as Co. clerk he was CP2's platoon clerk for over a year and excelled in the role.

2Lt. Kear said:
T/4 Burg, ... the background cornerstone that keeps Charlie's paperwork, AARs, awards etc. running behind the scenes for years now.

  • Amazing clerking and staff office attitude, worked consistently to a high standard in both capacities as PC and CC without complaint
  • Reliable and proactive, has never needed reminding of his duties and gone above and beyond his own to ensure slacking PC work was handled
  • Helps track important company metrics so that Co. HQ can make the best informed decisions regarding drill scheduling
  • Alongside clerking his duties in 8 staff offices are of a high calibre and demonstrated the best of non-combat 29th values
  • To round it all off: is a humble and committed member of the company and has never (to my knowledge) complained nor shirked his duties in all his years of service as a clerk in both company and platoon capacities combined.


With Battalion HQ approval, Charlie Company is proud to award T/4 Burg the Army Achievement Award for dedicated service and consistently performing his duties to the highest calibre.

Congratulations T/4!
