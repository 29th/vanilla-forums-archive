Six Years and Six Months of Service and Badge (Henry S. Lagenbrunner)

T4 Ceglar [Ret.]
April 2018 edited April 2018 in Promotions and Awards

Even before the big move, CW2 Lagenbrunner had a long and distinguished career, being involved in leadership roles in Darkest Hour and RO2. When it was decided that he would lead the newly formed Easy Company, he oversaw the successful adaptation of the 29th model to Squad. Subsequently, the new company had to grow in numbers - both to provide a healthy drill environment, and to prove its legitimacy. CW2 Lagenbrunner has personally participated in this effort by actively pubbing, recruiting and even instructing recruits. Today Easy Company is 100 strong, with two platoons and 7 full-size squads - and our CO has played no small role in its success.
