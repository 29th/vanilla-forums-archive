One Year of Service (Drake H. Perez Pantoja)

T5 Saunders
March 2020 edited April 2020 in Promotions and Awards

Sgt. Mellinger said:

There has been a lot of 29th members that have contributed a great amount of work in Easy Company. T/5 Perez-Pantoja is one of the people on top of this list. He has put countless hours working in the Squad SDK to create and maintain the mods that we use for drills. His mods have completely innovated the way we run drills. The mods have brought a lot of quality of life additions as well as content updates which include new emplacements, a Stryker MGS, a Barrett .50 Cal, an LWMMG, and the Marine Corps faction. T/5 PP also contributed a lot of work in Quartermaster before he discharged for a brief amount of time. His work is still being used today to help create our unit logos. Outside of his office jobs, he is one of the most feared guys in Easy Company. He was awarded a Bronze Star during EBOTs for decimating our previous squad. He quickly became one of the best Combat Engineers and is starting to become a great AR. What’s even more impressive is that he was able to accomplish in a span of a year. Thank you for all the coal you shoveled and congratulations on your first year in the unit.
