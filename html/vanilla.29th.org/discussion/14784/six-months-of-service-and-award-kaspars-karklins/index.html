Six Months of Service and Award (Kaspars Kārkliņš)

T4 Orhan
October 2016 edited October 2016 in Promotions and Awards

Cpl. Morten said:

Pvt. Kārkliņš have now been in the squad for 6 months and have become a crucial part of the squad and a guy we always can rely on to do the tasks we tell him to do to the best of his abilities. Since the day he joined, his willingness to learn have not diminished. He often comes asking me or Cpl Judge about what he could do to improve himself or wanting to learn some tricks to help him on the battlefield. Pvt. Kārkliņš have shown great skill with the rifle, begin able to hold a flank on his own and picking of enemies from far. Congratulations on your Six Months of Service Private! We are looking forward to seeing your forward development!


2Lt. Dashwood said:

As we all know, keeping the recruitment up is one of the most vital jobs a 29th member can have. Pvt. Kārkliņš decided to step up for it and took the initative. He worked on Battalion Server to make people aware of our unit. His solid work seems to paid off and he successfully managed to recruit TWO fresh recruits to 29th ID! For his excellent work on Recruitment, it is my pleasure to award him his 1st Combat Action Badge. Congratulations Pvt. Karklins and keep up the good work!
