Six Years of Service (Nickolas Hartstock)

PFC Moreno [Ret.]
November 2016 edited November 2016 in Promotions and Awards

SSgt. McDonough said:

SSgt. Hartstock has recently been transferred over to AP2 due to schedule conflicts and although he may be new to the platoon he is definitely a veteran of the unit. When I had learned that the SSgt. was getting up at 3AM nearly every platoon drill AP4 had I was amazed he was able to keep at it for so long. It goes to show the extreme dedication he is willing to put towards this unit and is a real testament to his character. We are still warming up to each other and learning each others strengths, weaknesses and what makes us tick but, I look forward to our continued work together within this unit and platoon. It brings me great pleasure to award SSgt. Hartstock his 12th AOCC and his 3rd WW1V medals, an impressive triple rainbow. Congrats SSgt, ever forward!
