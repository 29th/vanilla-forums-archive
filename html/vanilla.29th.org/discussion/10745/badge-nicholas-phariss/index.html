Badge (Nicholas Phariss)

PFC Rivero [Ret.]
April 2016 edited April 2016 in Promotions and Awards

PFC Warner said:

T4 Phariss' is a large, tall man. And yes, it is somewhat freakish and ironic that we shove such a large, tall man, coincidentally nicknamed "Fish," into a tank. Maybe it's a bit 'on the nose' with his illustrious career in the Navy. But I like to think it's because he lives in the desert wasteland of Las Vegas. I guess no one can really be sure.

The good Sgt. here was one of my DIs through BCT and because of that I have a lot to thank him for when it comes to my own development within the ranks of the 29th and Charlie. He is a wealth of knowledge, both useful and not. But in all seriousness, he is a good man and I am happy and proud to award his long overdue badge for Armor Marksman. Congratulations, T4.
*Salute*
