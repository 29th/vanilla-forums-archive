Promotion and Badges (Ricky L. Kyle)

T5 Valiūnas
December 2020 in Promotions and Awards

PFC Suárez said:

As an important member of the family, Pvt. Kyle has distinguished himself with all the rifles there are and what is yet to come. Understanding the importance of teamwork is vital for a squad and knowing when to shoot to coordinate with squad mates is not a simple task, and he has accomplished it to perfection. Following orders to a millimeter and volunteering to lead when asked to, able to hold flanks on his own, helping friendlies to locate enemy forces, even with the unreliable VOIP we currently have in-game are not easy tasks to perform. To me and everyone on DP1, you have shown to be a very capable Private and you are not a minesweeper anymore, being able to perform any tasks and volunteering for the most difficult tasks, it is my honor to promote you. Congratulations on your Rifle Marksmanship Badge, your Expert Infantry Badge and most important of all, your promotion to a Private, First Class. Congratulations Private, First Class Kyle!

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of

Ricky L. Kyle       012670786      DP1S1

I do promote him to Private, First Class in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 17th day of December 2020 .

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
