Awards (Charlie)

T4 Burg
March 2021 edited March 2021 in Promotions and Awards

FSgt. Balzer said:

The 29th Infantry Division conducted a friendly realism event with members of the 2nd Marine Raiders Battalion(2MRB). The 2MRB and the 29th have a long history together. Recently a realism event took place that not only pitted the 29th against the 2MRB, but also placed a large part of Charlie under the provisional field command of the 2MRB's 1Lt. Anderson. The spirit of competition and cooperation that have been the source of the relationship between the 29th and 2MRB was on full display. For their professionalism and participation in the second part of the event, these 29th members have earned themselves the Armed Forces Expeditionary Medal.



The following members of the 29th ID will receive the award:
CHQ
1Lt. Nelson

CP1:
T/5 Pinto
PFC B. Williams
PFC Hudson
PFC J. Gibson
