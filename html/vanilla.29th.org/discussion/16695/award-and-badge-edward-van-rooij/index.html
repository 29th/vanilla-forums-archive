Award and Badge (Edward van Rooij)

T4 Burg
February 2017 in Promotions and Awards

PFC McConville said:

Pvt. Van Rooij joined Charlie Company 5 months ago and in that time has done great things as a Pvt. He has shown just how much you can do at this rank and in an inspiration for all new members. He has of his own accord offered and lead optional drills to all those willing to put in the effort of learning that bit extra. His skills at using his rifle to full effect, and ability to share that with his fellow squad members has been a success! I always believe this extra effort must be acknowledged and so it is with great honour and respect that I award Pvt. Van Rooij his Rifle Marksman AQB! Truly deserved! Congratulations Pvt. Van Rooij!

FSgt. Balzer said:

On top of the Rifle Marksman AQB, Pvt. van Rooij also has put a lot of time and effort to teach and help others to make them better soldiers on the battlefield. Therefor Pvt. van Rooij will also be awarded a Good Conduct medal, Congratulations Pvt. van Rooij.
