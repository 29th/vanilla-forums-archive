Company of Heroes 1 - New Total Conversion mods added to Steam

PFC Krussnik [Ret.]
September 2016 edited January 2018 in Other Games

One of the best RTS games of all time.
Fan favorite mods have been added to the Steam Store Page for the first time.

Total conversions available on Steam for COH1 are also listed on the Legacy Edition page.

Full Mod Listing: http://store.steampowered.com/app/314420/

Individual Mods

  • Company of Heroes: Blitzkrieg Mod
    http://store.steampowered.com/app/681590/
    Released: 30 Nov, 2017
    Blitzkrieg mod is a modification for Company of Heroes which focuses on PvP experience. The mod aims for making the gameplay experience more realistic.

  • Modern Combat
    http://store.steampowered.com/app/341300/
    Released: 30 Nov, 2017
    An ambitious total-conversion modification for Relic's award-winning Company of Heroes®, Modern Combat transports players into the midst of a high-tech war between the modern-day People's Liberation Army and United States Armed Forces.

  • Company of Heroes: Battle of Crete
    http://store.steampowered.com/app/721720/
    Released: 27 Oct, 2017
    Battle of Crete is a modification for Company of Heroes. Mod based on real historical event during WW2 between May 20 and June 1 1941 as known as "Operation Mercury".

  • Company of Heroes: Eastern Front
    http://store.steampowered.com/app/317600/
    Released: 1 Jun, 2017
    Eastern Front is a free mod for Company of Heroes which has successfully added a full Soviet faction packed with unique and exciting units as well as the rugged Ostheer, a unique new Axis faction which was designed to counter the Soviets, a feat never before achieved in the Company of Heroes world.

  • Far East War
    http://store.steampowered.com/app/547180/
    Released: 12 Dec, 2016
    The Second Sino-Japanese War is part of the World War II, but this brutal war in Asia was, for a long time, forgotten by the world. "Far East War" is a modification based on "Company of Heroes", with a focus on the Second Sino-Japanese War, with new factions and units.

  • Europe at War
    http://store.steampowered.com/app/342370/
    Released: 18 May, 2015
    EaW is a historical mod for Company of Heroes that adds new battlefronts with new commander trees, abilities, units, vehicles and weapons for each, representing in the best way, as far as possible, historical accuracy, to immerse players in a much more realistic gaming experience.

  • The Great War 1918
    http://store.steampowered.com/app/314420/
    Released: 21 Aug, 2014
    TGW 1918 is a WW1 modification for Company of Heroes, focusing on the last year of the First World War. Currently it features the British Expeditionary Force and the German Empire as opposing factions; however new nations will be brought over time, such as France and Austria-Hungary.
