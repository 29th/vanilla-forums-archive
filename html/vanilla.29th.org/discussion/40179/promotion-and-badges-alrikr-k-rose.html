Promotion and Badges (Alrikr K. Rose)

T3 Chandonnet
September 2020 in Promotions and Awards

PFC Winston said:

Recently, Pvt. Rose has really stepped up. The transformation that he has undergone over the recent months from a relatively quiet member of the squad to a consistently positive soldier who continuously demonstrates his dedication to not only the squad, but also to the 29th as a whole, speaks volumes of his character and as a star member of the squad. With every drill he is in, Pvt. Rose demonstrates cohesiveness with his squad and teammates as well as his individual skills as a player. Every time I ask something of Rose during drills I have full confidence that he will perform it exceptionally and without fail. Rose has one of the highest guesting rates in DP2S1, and he is a consistently present member of the squad, always sitting in the Teamspeak interacting with other members of the 29th. Rose’s growing dedication to the 29th as a whole more than qualifies him for PFC, so congrats PFC Rose on achieving your trifecta!

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of

Alrikr K. Rose       332066903      DP2S1

I do promote him to Private, First Class in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 9th day of September 2020 .

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
