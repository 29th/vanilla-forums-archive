Award (Joris Corrion)

CW4 Slomczynski
May 2017 in Promotions and Awards

Cpt. Patty said:

Sgt. Corrion stepped up into mapping with no prior experience. With an eagerness to learn and the drive to carry out his ideas. He has both mastered the complex (and somewhat clunky) Unreal 2.5 editor AND created no less 5 brand new maps and ported a dozen broken 5.0 maps into 6.0 and 7.0 versions of DH.

This is an outstanding achievement, I would not have expected someone starting from scratch to even master the editor in such a short time, not to mention going on to fix handfuls of broken maps, AND THEN make Five Totally Brand New maps which are both beautiful, well polished and amazingly fun to play.

Not only teaching himself, Sgt. Corrion has also taught two other mappers who have gone on to produce new maps of their own.

For consistently performing his duties in a staff position to the highest caliber I am happy to award Sgt. Corrion Army Achievement Award. Congratulations Sgt!
