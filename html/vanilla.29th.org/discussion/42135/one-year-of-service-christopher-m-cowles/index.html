One Year of Service (Christopher M. Cowles)

T3 Chandonnet
December 2020 in Promotions and Awards

PFC Winston said:

Despite only being around for one year, the impact that PFC Cowles has had on not just Dog Company, but the 29th as a whole, has been one that is rarely seen in the history of the 29th. First and foremost, PFC Cowles exemplifies the qualities of what a rifleman and pilot strives to be, and presents an expert level of player skill and game knowledge rarely seen in anyone. And if DP2S1 were to not have him, we would be sorely missing one of the key components to how the squad functions. He is also one of the highest guesters in the squad as well, and it is a rare sight to not see him on the guest team every night. And his achievements don’t stop there, as he basically runs Dog’s Lighthouse as well, as he holds three separate positions in just Lighthouse alone. To add on to his 29th jobs, he is also a member of Dog’s camera crew, so we can all see him rack up kills on the scoreboard! He is also one of the most exemplary recruiters in the 29th for not just Dog Company, but also Easy Company as well. And as of now, he has a total of 68 recruits, which is a number that grows each day. To add on top of that, PFC Cowles is seeking to become a future leader in the 29th, and if his past achievements are anything to go by, he’s going to go far in that area as well. To conclude, PFC Cowles is one of the most important members of DP2S1, and I couldn’t even imagine what the 29th would be without him. So congrats on your one year PFC, I am proud to have you in my squad, and even prouder to call you my friend.
