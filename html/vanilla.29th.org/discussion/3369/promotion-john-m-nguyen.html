Promotion (John M. Nguyen)

TSgt. Wimpfheimer
April 2015 edited April 2015 in Promotions and Awards

2Lt. Boughen said:
"Over the years, I've seen very many leaders, but very few who possess the intellectual prowess of PFC Nguyen. His quick wit has profited him well as ASL of S2. He has learned quickly, and has continually been a font of thoughtfulness that has helped drive the platoon, as well as his squad, onwards. I can only wish that his doctrines continue to grace the 29th for a long while yet, and his presence too in AP2S2. It is my pleasure to promote him to the rank of Corporal. Congratulations Cpl. Nguyen!"

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of


John M. Nguyen     76561198014444239     AP2S2

I do promote him to Corporal in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 29th day of April 2015.

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army. Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
