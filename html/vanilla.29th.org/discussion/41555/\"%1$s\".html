Corporal Falenty, also known as that strange character with the mustache, has been with us since 2018. Ever since then, this Corporal has been striving to get the best out of the 29th. Eager as he is he stepped up to the plate and joined Squad Leader Training, which he finished last July. Under his command the second squad has been thriving and been fighting like a fierce machine on the field, coming second in Charlie Company Battle of the Squads and leaving the field of honor with their heads high.
It is more than a pleasure to have Corporal Falenty with us as a solid rock, a true Charlie NCO. Reliable, devastating, eager and fierce.
With this being said, it is more than an honor to award Corporal Falenty his fourth Army Of Occupation Medal and his first World War I Victory Medal, for his dedicated service within the 29th Infantry Division. Welcome to the Rainbow Club Corporal.
