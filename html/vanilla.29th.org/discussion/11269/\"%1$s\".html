Badge (Kenneth Lindholm)

Cpl. Saerens [Ret.]
April 2016 in Promotions and Awards

2Lt. Fritz said:

PFC Lindholm is a crack shot. We all know this. Many of us have fell under his murderous gaze as he zealously picked off our friends and ourselves during drills. And although this could be said about many of our players in the 29th Infantry Division, PFC Lindholm has been known to having grown a fond attachment to his rifle. Over the past many months, he's dedicated hours to the study of how a perfect rifleman should operate in a variety of situations. He was awarded a Bronze Star back in November for prowess on the battlefield, and since, he's been perfecting his skills and sharing them with those in AP1 during his Rifle lessons, of which many are benefiting from. Because of his determination and dedication, and the raw talent he posses, he is being awarded the Rifle Sharpshooter badge and being moved up to the position of Platoon Sniper. Congratulations PFC on this monumental stepping stone and keep the kills coming.
