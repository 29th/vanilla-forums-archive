Two Years and Six Months of Service (Lars Burg)

SSgt. Meyberg [Ret.]
October 2016 edited October 2016 in Promotions and Awards

I've know T/5 Burg for a long time now, He was already with the 29th when I joined 6 Years ago. At the time T/5 Burg had already picked up the MG AIT and made sure we all knew about it(Mostly Killing us with it). Now, Some years later, I have the pleasure to serve with him again and where would I be without him. He is one of the most important people in my Platoon as a Clerk. I hope you will stay with us for a long time T/5 Burg!

Congratulation on your 5th AOCC
