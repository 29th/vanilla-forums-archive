Four Years of Service (Ross M. Dethfield)

T4 Ceglar [Ret.]
October 2019 in Promotions and Awards

1Lt. Fritz said:

It's been over 4 years since I made the regretful decision to recruit some pubber named Dethfield on the sands of Iwo Jima. You would have thought the sands were salt as even when he was going through his second enlistment process, he was hooting and hollering about how things could be done better. I initially thought he'd get denied but 4 years later, he's still here. This time, his distaste of the sun and sand has expanded to improving many of the little things that makes this place run. Love or hate him (and if you're in Easy Co., it's more or less mandatory that you love him), his four years of service have been influential in raising or influencing leadership, instilling his style into each and every one of us. Congrats on your double rainbow and thanks for all you do old man.
