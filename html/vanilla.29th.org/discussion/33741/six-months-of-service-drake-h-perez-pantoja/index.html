Six Months of Service (Drake H. Perez-Pantoja)

Maj. Dethfield
August 2019 edited August 2019 in Promotions and Awards

Cpl. Sutera said:

Can you actually believe that this guy has only been here 6 months. He has made his way around here faster than a bad flu. This guy has won the Superior Unit Citation while in EP2S2. Won a CAB after just 2 months. Joined the Engineer Corps in and worked tiresly on the mods we all love and abuse. He has put in hours and hours of work with his AIT as a CE and landed rocket shots thought impossible. He then recieved a Good Conduct Medal for his work as well as a CAB 2 for his recruiting. And if all of this wasnt enough, he went on to recieve a Bronze Star for his efforts during Ep2S4's first EBOTS match. He then proceeded to help grow the 29th further by recruiting 10 people into the unit and recieve a CAB 3. He's also got a CE marksman badge. But we all could have guessed that. Hard to believe this is just for his first AOCC. This has been a long list of his accomplishments but thats just what this is for. In just 6 months, he has a portfolio here that rivals people with years of experience. PP, for all your work, we all appreciate it and thank you for what you do. Congrats on your first 6 months here and I look forward to the next 6.
