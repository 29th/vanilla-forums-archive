Operation Exodus Angel 		 

WARNO


1). Situation:

NATO forces have illegally encroached on Chenarus sovereignty. The Chenarus Provisional Government(provisional iNation member), have asked for protection. Civilians in Elektrozavodsk are being herded on ships and are being forcibly relocated. With NATO air assets neutralized by our defenses, we are preparing a armored push to the coast to protect the civilian population of Elektrozavodsk. Charlovi Battalion is tasked with supporting this move.

2). Mission:

iNation Armored armored forces will be moving south from Mogilevka towards Elektrozavodsk. Our armor is being prepped just south west of Mogilevka for an attack that should be ready in the next few days. Move south and clear the path for our armor. A local militia Checkpoint has been spotted near 098,107. The militia force has placed anti tank mines on the roadway on or near the Checkpoint. Local militia forces are known to be hostile to both iNation and NATO forces. Charlovi Battalion is tasked with capturing and clearing the mines at or neat the checkpoint. It is also expected NATO forces will be moving North to stop or slow our advance. Destroy the mines at check point. Also, NATO forces are preparing anti armor positions north of Elektrozavodsk. Destroy these positions.

  • 1). Take and hold the Checkpoint at 098,107
  • 2). Defend our armor assets near 082,104
  • 3). Destroy anti armor assets at or near 102,123


3). General Instructions.

A). Organization/Command:

Charlovi Battalion in over all command of Opfor

. Weapons and Equipment:

Unlimited 5.56/5.45 faction specific rifles with standard optics (ACOG/RCO or less)
2X faction correct MGs
2X Medics
1X Faction correct Single Shot AT launcher (RsH-26)
1X Faction correct Multi Launcher 4 rounds total
2X GLs - 3 rd GLs are banned
2X 7.62 Rifle with standard optics (ACOG/RCO or less)
1X Sniper/marksman

  • No Thermals - None, no gun optics with thermals or binoc thermals.
  • 2 Silencers for Opfor.
  • No guided Launchers.
  • Range finders with night vision for Squad Leaders and above ONLY.
  • All others may have binocs.
  • NVGs are allowed.
  • Satchel Explosives are allowed (6 total).
  • Reasonable and realistic kit, ammo, loadouts,etc.

D). Intelligence:

Hostile Forces:

Local Militia Forces are known to be hostile to both iNation and NATO forces. They blame both for the conflict in Chenarus. Made up of retired Armed forces of Chenarus members, local police and other paramilitary groups, they are assumed to be small in numbers and loosely organized. Lightly armed with Soviet era equipment, this small force should be considered competent and motivated.

NATO forces recently encountered were of the 2nd MRB, supported by elements of Bravo Company. This is a US Marine Recon force equipped with first rate US Kit.

    04:10 Z 29 MAY 16

    1). Situation:

    iNation forces will be making their first moves towards establishing sovereign territory. With enemy communications compromised, NATO naval assets have been mostly ineffective. If the airbase on Stratis can be eliminated, NATOs air dominance of the eastern Mediterranean will be severely limited. To this end, Charlovi Battalion will land forces to establish a beachhead and neutralize several communication towers on the island. Coordinated with this will be an attack on NATO communications via our CyInt group. Once communications have been severed with the main land, the siege of the lightly defended airbase can commence. The neutralization of the Stratis Air Base will make future actions possible.

    2). Mission:

    Primary mission is to destroy (3) communication towers, Code Named John Deere, Kubota and Yanmar. Sufficient demolitions will be provided for the accomplishment of this mission. (2) Additional towers, Code Named Tecumseh and New Holland, flank the larger objective tower Yanmar. Objectives Tecumseh and New Holland are secondary targets. Destruction of the enemy forces that may be defending the towers is also secondary to the destruction of the (3) main target towers.
    1). Primary - Destroy (3) communication towers 1, 2, & 4 at 3 separate sites.
    2). Secondary - (2) communication towers 3 & 5 near tower 3.
    3). Elimination of enemy forces in the area of operation.

    Charlovi Battalion will be inserted on the south of Stratis. While Charlovi 1 establishes a FOB at point XXXX, Charlovi 2 will move north and destroy (3) communication towers.
