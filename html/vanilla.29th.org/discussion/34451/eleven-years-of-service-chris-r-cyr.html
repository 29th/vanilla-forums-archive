Eleven Years of Service (Chris R. Cyr)

Technician, 4th Grade Adam K. Stachurski
October 2019 in Promotions and Awards

2Lt. Ochs said:

Many of you know the man, myth, and legend leading Dog Company, FSgt. Cyr. I have known him from my time in Able and now in Dog. After Able closed down he came to Dog and took over running things. This man is a very old man in 29th time and has given many years to the unit, 11 to be exact. This incredible amount of time is the reason he is receiving his 22nd AoCC for his 11 years of service to the 29th. Congrats FSgt. Cyr on this very impressive milestone that few have reached!
