Eight Years of Service and Badge (Daniel A. Balzer)

T5 Vonk [Ret.]
April 2017 edited April 2017 in Promotions and Awards

CW3 Hill said:

"Time; it has a way of seeming fleeting. We give it freely to the things we care about, covet it when we think we won't have enough, and beg for it when we want just the smallest bit more of it.

FSgt. always seems to find the right amount of time. The right amount of time to listen to a new Private. The right amount to give advice to a Platoon Sergeant. The right amount to give praise to a Squad Leader. FSgt. is always willing to share his personal share of time with us and the 29th and we are grateful. Grateful for his wisdom and knowledge, grateful for his patience and foresight, grateful for his wit and candor.

For over 8 years FSgt. Balzer has scratched, pushed, reasoned, shouted, pleaded, praised, whispered, and advised his way through the, sometimes tumultuous but always fun, second family we call the 29th. FSgt. is a pillar of the 29th; a stalwart champion for the men in his charge.

To FSgt. Balzer, I say thank you. Thank you for helping build and maintain the 29th for these many years. Thank you for building and maintaining Charlie Company. Most of all thank you for being my friend and advisor; I simply could not do this thing we do without you

Congratulations FSgt. Balzer on 8 years of service to the 29th, your 16th AOCC and 4th WWI Victory Medal a quadruple rainbow.

Join me in a Salute to one of the most dedicated members to ever grace the ranks of the 29th.

Also, with time in comes knowledge and skill in the tools at your disposal. FSgt. has had the skill and knowledge for a long time and also the checkmarks but for some reason was not awarded. I will now remedy this by awarding FSgt. Balzer the Rifle Sharpshooter AQB. Congratulations FSgt.!"
