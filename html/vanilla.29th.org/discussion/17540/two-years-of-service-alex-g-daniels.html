Two Years of Service (Alex G. Daniels)

T4 Orhan
April 2017 edited April 2017 in Promotions and Awards

Sgt. Ternby said:

PFC Daniels is one of two MG's is in S1 and has been wrecking havoc with that AIT for a total of around 1 year and 4 months. While he's evolved greatly in his AIT during that time, and become a very skilled MG:er, SLT is where his focus lies at the moment. Having been placed into SLT almost one year ago, the PFC quickly showed that he was the right man from the job. He's come a long way on his path towards finishing SLT. At the start he was a bit nervous about commanding the squad and giving out orders, which is completely normal for fresh SLT'ers. He quickly grew out of it, however, and is today a decisive leader when it comes to decision making. At the same time he shows great respect and understanding for each individual soldier, their skills and AITs and often gives them some freedom when it comes to how they maneuver. This is very important when you command a veteran squad, since you utilize all the skills that each individual soldier possess. PFC Daniels is progressing really well and is overall a great guy and an excellent 29th member and I'm very glad to have him in S1. He's now been here for a total of Two Years which is a very important milestone that makes him eligible for his Fourth Army of Occupation and First World War I Medals! Congratulations PFC Daniels!
