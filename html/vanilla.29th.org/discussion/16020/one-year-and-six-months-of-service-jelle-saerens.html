One Year and Six Months of Service (Jelle Saerens)

T5 Borkowski
December 2016 in Promotions and Awards

Sgt. Barclay said:

T/5 Saerens has come a long way in the last six months. In the prior six months he's gone from killing his squad leader with friendly grenades and having squad mates running in fear when he possessed the combat engineer roll to earning his combat engineer roll and his squad leader no longer shaking in his boots when he pulls out a grenade. He has proven himself as a reliable platoon clerk, become a senior recruitment officer, take on the role of tactical technician, and earned his SLT ribbon. In the last month he sadly gave up his clerk duties to become the ASL of AP1S3. It is with great pride I present T/5 Saerens with his third AOCC and I am looking forward to what he is going to do in the next six months.
