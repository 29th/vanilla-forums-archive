Two Years of Service (Petar Miochevich)

T5 Borkowski
July 2017 in Promotions and Awards

PFC Carbonneaux said:

Two months ago, I remember being here at the same place awarding, at the time, PFC Miochevich, his Marksman AQB for the Submachine gun. Well, here I am again to congratulate now Cpl. Miochevich getting his 4th Army of Occupation Medal and World War I Victory Medal! So, to an excellent leader and an excellent soldier, congratulations, Cpl.! To many more!
