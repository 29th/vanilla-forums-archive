Six Years and Six Months of Service (Dan Balzer)

T4 Phariss [Ret.]
August 2015 in Promotions and Awards

Fsgt. Hill said:
TSgt. Balzer was the sinew of CP1 for a long time; now he is the very marrow in Charlie. A stubborn man with a predilection for greatness, not sated by half measures or uncorrected failure. His influence could be felt throughout Charlie Company when he was an SL and has only increased as the years progressed. I am happy to have come to know him and consider him as a partner and a friend in running a company. I consider his efforts and dedication second to none. Not many realize how unflinching he is defense of and support for the men in Charlie Company. 6.5 years, 13 AOCCS are a testament to not just years of service but to perseverance and dedication. Congratulations TSgt.. Ever Forward!
