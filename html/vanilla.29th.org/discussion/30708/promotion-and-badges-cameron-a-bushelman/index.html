Promotion and Badges (Cameron A. Bushelman) 

T3 Grimes [Ret.]
March 2019 in Promotions and Awards

Cpl. Gibson said:

I must say that I feel like I am the luckiest squad leader ever. I recently was put in charge of the brand new EP3S4. This was not my first experience being a part of leadership in a freshly made squad. In fact it was my third. It was however the easiest I thought. The quality of members that I received was truly unbelievable. A prime example of this great pool of talent is Pvt. Bushelman. I know every word I write pains his former SL Cpl. Crane, and I am perfectly fine with that, because this private is fantastic and I can't wait to see what is in store for him. So thank you Pvt. Bushelman, along with the others, on making the creation of S4 so easy. Because of this excellence on your part, I feel I owe you. I think fair compensation would be a Rifle Marksman Badge and an Expert Infantry Badge. Great job! Oh and lets start calling you PFC now...

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of

Cameron A. Bushelman       070854828      EP3S4

I do promote him to Private, First Class in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 19th day of March 2019 .

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
