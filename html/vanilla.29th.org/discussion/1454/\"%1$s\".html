Honorable Discharge (Christopher D. Glancy)

Cpt. Walker [Ret.]
February 2015 in Discharges



HONORABLE DISCHARGE
FROM THE ARMED FORCES OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA


This is to certify that

CHRISTOPHER D. GLANCY    Private, First Class  Reserves

was Honorably Discharged from the

UNITED STATES ARMY

on the  11TH     day of     FEBRUARY 2015  . This certificate is awarded
as a testimonial of Honest and Faithful Service





ARPC-OPM-O

DATE: 2015 FEB 05

MEMORANDUM FOR Commander,
U.S. Army 29th Infantry Division, ATTN: AHRC-OP-S, 1 Reserve Way
Fort Belvoir, VA 22060

SUBJECT: Unqualified Resignation

  1. I hereby tender my unqualified resignation as an Enlisted Soldier in the United States Army under the provisions of Chapter 6, Section III, AR 135-175.

  2. I understand that if my resignation is accepted I am entitled to an honorable separation and will be furnished an Honorable Discharge Certificate. I understand that I cannot resign if I am a statutory obligor.


Christopher D. Glancy
Signature


Christopher D. Glancy
Name (Type or Print)


Private, First Class       Asst. Drill Instructor       Reserves
Rank       Position       Platoon/Squad



*Lack of Time to Commit

Boys, and girls, of the 29th infantry division, it's been an honor serving. I tried my best to interact with all of you individually. Here's what I remember about each of you, the best I can.

Bergstrom- First squad leader, made sure I knew everything I needed to know. Didn't take any crap.
Phariss- My pal, my buddy, my first real friend in the 29th. Trained me in tanking and made me realize how awesome the M5 is.
Blouin- Another buddy of mine. Good times were had in Men of War, as well as drills.
Anto- (Sorry about not spelling your name)-I found you immature at first, to be honest, and I think it's because you acted just like me. You turned out to be a good lead in the end- best of luck in the future.
Rousse- My second true friend in the 29th- someone I could truly talk to. Someone I could talk into a pringles can to and they would understand.
Morse- My third true friend and fellow cat. You left for a time, made me grumpy like Tardar (Grumpy Cat)
Morra- Oh Captain, my Captain. Had a company supported by a Stuart tank chase me through town on my first drill. Good man. Best of luck, sir. o7
Balzer- My first Platoon Sarge, and a good man- always had time to put up with my crap even if he didn't have that kind of time.
Mills- One of those cool COs that would come to you and talk to you about life.
Chev- The one who gave me a chance. Thank you. You were an eye-opener. (You should know what I mean.)
Savvy- My recruiter, the one that started this story.....and my last Platoon Sergeant.

The rest of you, you've each shown me that someone like me can join a community and be himself without being bullied, which I faced on a constant basis in high school. You've all worked together and made a serious community. The reasons I leave are not because of these, but I feel it may just be my time to move on.

Reason For Leaving
