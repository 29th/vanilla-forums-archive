Meritorious Public Service Medal (First Battalion)

CSM Conrad
April 2017 in Promotions and Awards

In recognition of their dedication to the unit through providing recurring financial contributions for consistent upkeep, the following members are being awarded the Meritorious Public Service Medal. This medal will remain on their jackets while they continue to provide contributions to the unit. Previous recipients of this medal who have maintained their contributions will retain the medal.

PFC Bae
FSgt. Balzer
T/4 Kabeya
Sgt. Kear
WO1 Littlefield
PFC Milo
Cpl. Neve
Sgt. Robles
PFC Willis
Sgt. Wimpfheimer

The entire unit as well as the Lt. Col. thank you for your generosity. These donations help pay for the expensive forum and server costs. Every donation is no doubt greatly appreciated.
