Award (Joshua S. Evarts)

T5 Saunders
March 2020 edited March 2020 in Promotions and Awards

Sgt. Mellinger said:

T/5 Evarts is someone who needs no introduction. He is one of the friendliest and one of the deadliest guys in Easy Company. I'm sure we have all been at the receiving end of his sniper rifle at one point or another. However, we are not necessarily looking at his performance in drills today. Today, we will be recognizing his ability to teach others. Throughout his time here, he has always been one to guest every drill he can. Not only does he guest, he will go out of his way and give feedback to people who are doing well or will give them some tips on how they can do better. Ever since T/5 Evarts was made the Platoon Sniper in EP2, he has made it his mission to make everyone else better so he can have some competition. He has run a total of 3 Rifleman Clinics since July of 2019. This is nothing to scoff at. It takes a lot of time and dedication to plan these clinics. Throughout all of them, he has been able to help many of rifleman in Easy Company including myself. This amount of work and depreciation is deserving of some reward so, we will be awarding T/5 Evarts a Good Conduct Medal. Thanks for all the tips and tricks you have taught us.
