Promotion (Christian D. Robles)

T4 Ceglar [Ret.]
November 2018 in Promotions and Awards

2Lt. Dashwood said:

SSgt. Robles is a man that needs no introduction. A veteran NCO from Able Company and EP3's own Platoon Sergeant. SSgt. Robles is a fine example of our EST leadership, always striving for excellence, not only showing it, but demanding it from his subordinates. In the ever-constant push to expand Easy Company, EP3 has received additional support which now puts SSgt. Robles in a position of educator to new HQ members, as such, his new responsibilities demand a new rank to denote the level of trust we have in him for leading his platoon. Further, I seek to continue CW2 Lagenbrunner's quest for world domination by expanding the Easy Co. Warrant Officer Army.
Therefore, it is with the GREATEST of pleasure that I hereby promote SSgt. Robles to the rank of Warrant Officer, First Class. Congratulations Mr. Robles.

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of

Christian D. Robles       109051248       EP3 HQ

I do promote him to Warrant Officer 1 in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 29 day of November 2018 .

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
