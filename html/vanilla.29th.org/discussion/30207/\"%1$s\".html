Three Years of Service (Ezequiel M. Alan) 

PFC Noonan
February 2019 in Promotions and Awards

Cpl. Mueller said:

PFC Alan has been in the 29th for 3 years now and in that time has been a model member. Back when he joined the unit as part of AP4S1, no one expected him to become the great MG user that he would eventually become, and along the way he earned his CAB 2 for his efforts at recruiting new members into the 29th. Not long ago, he joined us here in DP2S1, and, while you would expect the time zone differences to be an issue, PFC Alan has overcome them. Within 4 months of joining DP2S1, he earned his Rifle Marksmen badge and was assigned to the MG spot. Even more impressive is just 4 days past his 4th month in the squad as an MG, he received his MG Marksmen Badge. All in all, PFC Alan continues to impress, and as such I am glad to see his 6th AOCC. PFC Alan, you are one year away from joining the Rainbow Club. Keep up the great work.
