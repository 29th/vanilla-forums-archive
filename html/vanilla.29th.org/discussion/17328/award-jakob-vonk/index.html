Award (Jakob Vonk)

T4 Burg
March 2017 edited March 2017 in Promotions and Awards

Sgt. Kear said:

Following a recent Lighthouse restructure there is one staff member who has gone above and beyond his station to prove his dedication and worth to both the office and the unit overall. PFC Vonk, who at the time was a GMT ADI, stepped forward and committed himself time and time again to oversee the running of EST BCT sessions despite his own time-zone being GMT+1!

This meant staying up until almost 3am (his time) for five days in a row to fill in a crucial role during a recent shortage of manpower in A3 Lighthouse, despite this arduous task his tenacity, attitude and capabilities remained resolute and he is therefore worthy of receiving the Armed Forces Service Medal for going above and beyond the duties and responsibilities expected of a 29er in a staff position.
Congratulations PFC!
