Honorable Discharge (Mark A. Hartley)

CW4 Slomczynski
May 2017 edited May 2017 in Discharges

HONORABLE DISCHARGE

FROM THE ARMED FORCES OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA

This is to certify that

Mark A. Hartley     FSgt.     B HQ

was Honorably Discharged from the

UNITED STATES ARMY

on the 23rd day of May 2017. This certificate is awarded

as a testimonial of Honest and Faithful Service





ARPC-OPM-O

DATE: 23rd of May 2017

MEMORANDUM FOR Commander,

U.S. Army 29th Infantry Division, ATTN: AHRC-OP-S, 1 Reserve Way

Fort Belvoir, VA 22060


SUBJECT: Unqualified Resignation


1.I hereby tender my unqualified resignation as an Enlisted Soldier in the

United States Army under the provisions of Chapter 6, Section III,

AR 135-175.

2.I understand that if my resignation is accepted I am entitled to an

honorable separation and will be furnished an Honorable Discharge

Certificate. I understand that I cannot resign if I am a statutory obligor.


Signature
Mark A. Hartley
Name (Type or Print)
First Sergeant Senior NCO Baker Company HQ
Rank Position Platoon/Squad
I just want to say its been a privilege to be Company Sergeant of Baker Company and I have enjoyed every minute of it. However in the last few weeks certain events have made think towards RL, particularly my family and work. Although I could carry on, I feel now is the time to move on and let someone else have a go.

People to thank - well there are too many really to mention all great blokes, but I will single out a few. SSgts Kruihtof & Sterk - my two apprentices - I will miss you boys, and T/3 Swomma - the best clerk within the 29th - couldn't have done it without him; All the Platoon HQs - SSgts. Bagge and Kim and Noye, and WO1 Brewer, who I hope next time I call in has a bar on his shoulder! And Lts Derby and Dashwood, and finally Cpt. Patty - I know it's tough running outside company but if you get the right bloke in to replace me and you'll be right. It has been a pleasure working with you, Captain.

So to Sgt. Corrion and all the rest of the boys within the squads - look after yourselves and maybe one day I'll see you on the other side. Enjoy!

*Salute*
Reason for leaving
