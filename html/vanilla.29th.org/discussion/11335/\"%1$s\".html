Promotion (Alexander H. Lauritsen)

CSM Conrad
April 2016 edited April 2016 in Promotions and Awards

FSgt. Conrad said:

T/5 Lauritsen has always been a source of constant disappointment and is one of the worst people I've ever known. No matter how heavy of a workload is dumped on him, he is always complaining that we aren't doing enough to challenge him and insists on pestering Company HQ for extra work. He is unbearably punctual and hard working and expects everyone to have the same work ethic, especially those that he trains. He will never allow anyone a moment of peace or rest until his paperwork is just right. Fortunately for the good T/5, these awful traits make him quite an adequate clerk. In order to further distance him and his behavior from the less senior members of the 29th, we are promoting him to the rank of Technician, 4th Grade.

Congratulations T/4 Lauritsen.

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of

Alexandra H. Lauritsen      1197995097182     AP1S1

I do promote him to Technician, 4th Grade in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 24th day of April 2016.

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
