Award (Bruce L. Verbruggen)

CW4 Slomczynski
October 2016 in Promotions and Awards

Cpt. Patty said:

When the Darkest Hour 6.0 update came about and broke the mutators this could of literally been the end of DH drills as we knew them, indeed it could of been the end of DH being a viable game to drill on.

With all the famous names who made the mutators no longer in the unit it seemed we would be doomed to revert to a time we had not been in since 2009/10, a dark era of no mutators for DH.

However with no prior experience in DH modding or coding, PFC Verbruggen learnt as he went, using his own time and initiative to fix the broken mutators within weeks. As a relatively unknown Private at the time he went above and beyond what was expected of him. Not just fixing our crucial cMenu, paradrop and mine disabling mutators once, but fixing them repeatedly after the 6.0, 6.01 and 7.0 updates broke them again and again.

He also worked hard to fix other mutators such as salute, scoreboard and the custom uniforms, and though these projects have since been taken up by the returning T/4 Hopkins he made good progress and his enthusiasm and initiative was been duly noted.

Though his history of engineering in the 29th may not be as long and impressive as other names in the unit, he was there for Baker in it's hour of need, and the sole person responsible for allowing us to carry on drilling in DH in the way we have done for many years now.

For performing his duties above and beyond what was required of him, I am pleased to award PFC Verbruggen Armed Forces Service Medal. Congratulations PFC!
