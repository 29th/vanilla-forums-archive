Promotion and Badge (Selwyn v. Sluys)

Cpl. Saerens [Ret.]
April 2016 edited April 2016 in Promotions and Awards

Sgt. Part said:

Pvt. Sluys has proven to me in a very short time that he is the man for the job, if i need something to be done. He has great attendance which means he is always there to get the job done. He is an exemplary Private and has become a role-model for other Privates by recruiting 2 people earning himself CAB 1 and is eagerly awaiting his two next recruits to pass the ongoing BCT. He is not afraid to ask me questions, which means he is higly interested and want to constantly improve. Not only does he have a great attendance but he also put a lot of time into pubbing and helping us fill up our servers. I believe Pvt. Sluys is mature enough and ready to not only recieve his EIB but also to be promoted to the rank of PFC. Congratulations!

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of


Selwyn v. Sluys     044765955     Private

I do promote him to Private, First Class in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 07th day of APRIL 2016.

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
