Three Years of Service (Patrick H. Tilley)

T3 Chandonnet
November 2020 in Promotions and Awards

Cpl. Nguyen said:

I've said it countless times, Cpl. Tilley and I started from the same root in the 943rd Training Platoon a little more than three years ago. It's quite remarkable, really, to see him persist through all this time, going from an excellent machine gunner, great pilot, my dedicated ASL, to now where I'm his ASL and Cpl. Tilley the SL of the great squad of DP2S2. It's remarkable in the sense that the 29th has been such a big part of our life and Cpl. Tilley has been such a big part of DP2S2 and DP2 overall. We often talk of the old guards like 2Lt. Ochs, PFC Le, T/5 Chandon, PFC Ramsey, PFC Leonhardt, etc. who were there when Dog Company was revived and DP2S2 was reinstated. However, with three years under his belt in the same squad through thick and thin, Cpl. Tilley can truly join the ranks of them and continue the great leadership tradition of DP2S2. Quite a journey, and congratulations, Corporal!
