Promotion &amp; Badges (Kaspars Kārkliņš)

T4 Orhan
November 2016 edited November 2016 in Promotions and Awards

Cpl. Judge said:

Pvt. Kārkliņš has been a great soldier to work with over the last 6 months - although he started off quite quiet and unsure of himself, he was always looking to improve himself by asking the right questions on how he could improve. He is a private who genuinely wants what's best for his squad, and wants to do what he can to help. More recently, he has become incredibly consistent with his rifle and general techniques, and can really be counted on to do his job. As well as becoming a decent fighter, he has become a good team-player, with excellent communication skills. We feel that the private should be rewarded for all of his hard work; he certainly deserves not only his EIB and Rifle Marksman Badge, but also a promotion to the more fitting rank of Private First Class. Congratulations PFC!

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of

Kaspars Kārkliņš      155879424     BP1S2

I do promote him to Private, First Class in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 10th day of October 2016.

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
