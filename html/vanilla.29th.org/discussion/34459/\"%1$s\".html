Promotion and Badges (Alexander Boots) 

T5 Perez Pantoja
October 2019 in Promotions and Awards

Cpl. Newton said:

There was a lot of uncertainty in my mind for how EP4S3 would operate and how it's members would be when it was first created. Pvt. Boots has been a bit of certainty among the chaos from day 1. His attendance has been excellent, his rifle skills have been on point, his communication and teamwork have also been exactly what I look for from a member of my squad. His rifle skills even managed to earn him a rifle marksman badge and an AIT as a Pvt, which is no small feat. His conduct however is his most defining feature. Boots's presence is always made known the moment he joins the TS channel and is kept that way up until the moment he leaves with his constant off the wall references and inuendos each being more bizarre than the last. He brings a welcome amount of brevity and comedy to the squad, which I greatly appreciate. His conduct to this point is unbecoming of his rank and I feel it necessary to finally complete his trifecta of awards and award him his Expert Infantry Badge as well as promote him to the rank of PFC. Congratulations PFC Boots!

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of

Alexander Boots       198560130      EP4S3

I do promote him to Private, First Class in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 13th day of October 2019 .

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
