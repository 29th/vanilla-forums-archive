One Year of Service (Joseph D. Brophy) 

PFC Noonan
August 2019 in Promotions and Awards

PFC Greeves said:

For as long as I have known him, PFC Brophy has been an approachable and reliable brother-in-arms. As both a rifleman, a fellow PFC, and one of our first crewmen, he has shown great ease in his functions. Through his open-minded, easy-going, laid-back attitude, he has earned my respect and the respect of his peers amidst DP3. Although he appears aloof on the surface, he knows how to put his war face on when it is time to hit the ground, and he shows his professionalism on the field as a solid rifleman and a fearsome whirlybird operator. I am thankful to have been part of PFC Brophy's squad for this past year and to have grown alongside him. Here is hoping he keeps it up and grows the reputation of Crewmen as forces to be reckoned with! Congrats on your first year within the 29th and second AOCC, PFC!
