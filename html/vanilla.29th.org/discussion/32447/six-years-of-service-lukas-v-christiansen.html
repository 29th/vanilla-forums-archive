Six Years of Service (Lukas V. Christiansen) 

T5 Brown [Ret.]
June 2019 in Promotions and Awards

SSgt. Butler said:

Sgt. Christiansen is a man of mettle. A fusion of brains and brawn. A skilled tactician and, even though his decibel levels may at times fool you to think otherwise, a very patient leader. He's everything a good squad leader is. Knowledgeable and skilled in all the things he expects his squad to be, he leads by example. Always willing to give me a down to earth and honest perspective of the operations of CP2 and Charlie, I'm glad to have him as one of my squad leaders and his squad is lucky to have him. Having now spent a mental total of six years in the 29th, I've got some bling for him.

Congratulations on your 12th AOCC and 3rd WWIVM, Sergeant. See you at the next one.
