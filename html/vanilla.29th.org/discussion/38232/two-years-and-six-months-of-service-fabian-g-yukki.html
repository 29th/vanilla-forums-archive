Two Years and Six Months of Service (Fabian G. Yukki)

Technician, 4th Grade Adam K. Stachurski
June 2020 in Promotions and Awards

Cpl. Griffin said:

I seem to have had to write a lot about T/5 Yukki since becoming his SL last year, in the last 6 months he has achieved a lot, namely achieving a coveted Sharpshooter Badge for the SMG, to which he then recently displayed those hard earned skills and experiences in a flurry of small calibre violence and death, which resulted in him being awarded a Bronze Star! All the the while he also continues to serve diligently as our Platoon Clerk and a Senior Position with the Adjutant Corps. This is a Soldier who continues to serve the 29th with excellence on and off the field, congratulations on your 5th AOCC!
