Honorable Discharge (Joseph J. Chevarie)

T3 Lauritsen
April 2016 in Discharges


HONORABLE DISCHARGE

FROM THE ARMED FORCES OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA

This is to certify that

Joseph J. Chevarie    SSgt.    AP3HQ

was Honorably Discharged from the

UNITED STATES ARMY

on the 11th day of APRIL 2016. This certificate is awarded

as a testimonial of Honest and Faithful Service





ARPC-OPM-O

DATE: 11 APR 2016

MEMORANDUM FOR Commander,

U.S. Army 29th Infantry Division, ATTN: AHRC-OP-S, 1 Reserve Way

Fort Belvoir, VA 22060


SUBJECT: Unqualified Resignation


1.I hereby tender my unqualified resignation as an Enlisted Soldier in the

United States Army under the provisions of Chapter 6, Section III,

AR 135-175.

2.I understand that if my resignation is accepted I am entitled to an

honorable separation and will be furnished an Honorable Discharge

Certificate. I understand that I cannot resign if I am a statutory obligor.


Joseph J. Chevarie
Signature
Joseph J. Chevarie
Name (Type or Print)
SSgt. Platoon Sergeant Able Company, Third Platoon HQ
Rank Position Platoon/Squad

Hello Guys !

The passed months I have been an emptyshell in the 29th. I'm trying to finish my degree and publish my research while preparing for my upcoming job and with that, many changes occured in my life, making it too busy to spare the bare minimum time I should dedicate to the 29th with my current rank.

Thus, the time has come for me after 6 years to take my leave, at least temporary untill next september, and let the chance for people able of more dedication to lead AP3. Still, those 6 years have been wonderful among you guys. Every opportunities that were offered to me in and out of the battlefield along with every challenges I've overcome have molded and changed me to become what I am today. For that, I'll be ever grateful towards the 29th but this is only outmatched by how great you guys are and have been towards me! I've met so many awesome people in here that making an exhaustive list of them is absolutly impossible and even if I'd spend a day writing down all the fonds memories I have with every single one of you, passed and present members, that wouldn't be enough.

So with that said, I will only give you all a "thank you and farewell" from the bottom of my heart for those years with you. Ever Foward and Attaboy all the way !

- Chev
