Promotion (Leon D. Laird)

T5 Lorne
July 2018 in Promotions and Awards

2Lt. Kear said:

I cannot, on good conscience, recall, an instance where I have ever had to remind or request that a task be done: Corporal Laird is always conscientiously ahead of his duties, his constant proactivity makes him a joy to work with as one of CP2's squad leaders. His level of attention and care to those under him is evident in the way he comports himself both in battlefield decorum and through the multitudes of paperwork he never fails to submit in a timely and comprehensive manner.

Corporal Laird has proven time and time again that he is no longer fit for the rank he currently holds. During his tenure in the 29th he has displayed determination and steadfastness that epitomise the highest traditions of CP2 and it is a privilege to promote Cpl. Laird to the rank of Sergeant.

Congratulations.

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of

Leon D. Laird       081853113       CP2S3

I do promote him to Sergeant in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 29th day of July 2018 .

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
