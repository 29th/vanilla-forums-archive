Promotion (Oskar L. Jonasson)

T4 Burg
November 2016 edited November 2016 in Promotions and Awards

SSgt. Meyberg said:

PFC Jonasson has been with us for over 8 months now. In these 8 Months, he has grown from the average 29th recruit into an amazing leader who got Squad 3 back on track when they were in a big low. This was the moment for me where he would either break or make it as a leader but he showed us all that he is capable. I think you can ask any member of squad 3 how great of a leader PFC Jonasson is and they will all be able to tell you an incredible story about PFC Jonasson as a leader.

Unfortunately, due to RL reasons PFC Jonasson has to leave us as a Platoon but due to his hard work in Lighthouse he has the opportunity to go to the reserves, where he will be able to keep training the recruits for Charlie Company. But before I let you go PFC Jonasson, there is one last thing I get to do.
It’s an honour to promote PFC Jonasson to the rank of T/5.
Contgatulations T/5 Jonasson.

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of

Oskar L. Jonasson      018176852     CP2S3

I do promote him to Technician, 5th Grade in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 27th day of November 2016 .

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
