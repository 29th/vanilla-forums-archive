Promotion (Jessica Jess) 

T5 Gillespie
January 2021 edited February 2021 in Promotions and Awards

SSgt. Curran said:

There are a few individuals in the unit that upon viewing in game that you are immediately impressed, PFC Jess is one of them. Ever since joining the unit PFC Jess has made a name for her self with both her skills with a rifle and her ability to effectively mark and call out targets which and if you sat down and talked with her you'd understand why that is such a feet. I have witnessed the PFC clear objectives with only her rifle and a mean look, I have seen her give key reports that help our team push and take objectives with easy. Skill like this does not come around very often and I for one believe it should not go unrewarded, Congratulations T/5 Jess!

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of

Jessica Jess       449398586      EP5S2

I do promote her to Technician, Fifth Grade in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 28th day of January 2021 .

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
