Honorable Discharge (Nikolas Gerardo V. Valitutto)

T5 Borkowski
January 2018 in Discharges

HONORABLE DISCHARGE

FROM THE ARMED FORCES OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA

This is to certify that

Nikolas Gerardo V. Valitutto     Private, First Class     AP1S1

was Honorably Discharged from the

UNITED STATES ARMY

on the 22nd day of January 2018. This certificate is awarded

as a testimonial of Honest and Faithful Service





ARPC-OPM-O

DATE: 22 January 2018

MEMORANDUM FOR Commander,

U.S. Army 29th Infantry Division, ATTN: AHRC-OP-S, 1 Reserve Way

Fort Belvoir, VA 22060


SUBJECT: Unqualified Resignation


1.I hereby tender my unqualified resignation as an Enlisted Soldier in the

United States Army under the provisions of Chapter 6, Section III,

AR 135-175.

2.I understand that if my resignation is accepted I am entitled to an

honorable separation and will be furnished an Honorable Discharge

Certificate. I understand that I cannot resign if I am a statutory obligor.


Nikolas Gerardo V. Valitutto
Signature
Nikolas Gerardo V. Valitutto
Name (Type or Print)
Private, First Class Submachine Gunner AP1S1
Rank Position Platoon/Squad

This is my last message to the squad before my discharge, when I join in this fantastic unit, I fell like a soldier, real soldier. Here in this unit, i learn a lot of things, I improve my English, my communication, my skills. The 29 for me is a second family, I would not leave, but I have problems I need to study. but I will return here. I wish good luck to my team. Salute.
Reason for leaving
APPROVED
