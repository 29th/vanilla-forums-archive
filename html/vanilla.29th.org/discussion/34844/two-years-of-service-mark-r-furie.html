Two Years of Service (Mark R. Furie) 

T5 Gillespie
November 2019 edited November 2019 in Promotions and Awards

Cpl. Greene said:

Its crazy how fast time has flown. Since having Sgt. Furie take over command of S1 the squad has progressed leaps and bounds. Having had him as my assistant squad leader in my early days in the unit it's been amazing to watch how he has progressed since his days as a Cpl. He has been irreplaceable at transitioning to the command of S1 and has kept the squad operating at their fullest potential. It seems that the good Sgt. been able to keep the morale and cohesion of our squad at a consistent high. With that said I'm happy to present Sgt. Furie with his 4th Army of Occupation Medal! May his continued success lead him and S1 to further accomplishments in the 29th.
