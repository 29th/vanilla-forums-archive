Honorable Discharge (Eden Langford)

T3 Lauritsen
July 2016 edited July 2016 in Discharges


HONORABLE DISCHARGE

FROM THE ARMED FORCES OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA

This is to certify that

Eden Langford    Cpl.    AP3S4

was Honorably Discharged from the

UNITED STATES ARMY

on the 05th day of JULY 2016. This certificate is awarded

as a testimonial of Honest and Faithful Service





ARPC-OPM-O

DATE: 05 JUL 2016

MEMORANDUM FOR Commander,

U.S. Army 29th Infantry Division, ATTN: AHRC-OP-S, 1 Reserve Way

Fort Belvoir, VA 22060


SUBJECT: Unqualified Resignation


1.I hereby tender my unqualified resignation as an Enlisted Soldier in the

United States Army under the provisions of Chapter 6, Section III,

AR 135-175.

2.I understand that if my resignation is accepted I am entitled to an

honorable separation and will be furnished an Honorable Discharge

Certificate. I understand that I cannot resign if I am a statutory obligor.


Signature
Eden Langford
Name (Type or Print)
Cpl. Squad Leader Able Company, Third Platoon, Fourth Squad
Rank Position Platoon/Squad

Hello guys!

As most of you know, in the past month there's been major life changes happening for me. My new job on this cruise ship is keeping me away most of the time, leaving me very little time for activities and even less time to dedicate to the Unit.Real life comes first they say and this time I have to agree.

Time is flying by, isn't it? I've been in the Unit for almost two years and a half and I truly had a blast. Meeting new people, playing games and staying up late just to "hang out" were part of my every day life during this time and I will miss it for sure. I would like to thank you all for being such an awesome community, it honestly feels like a second family to me. Thank you for the countless hours in and out of game, all the laughter in TS and the great memories that I will keep in mind as the "good old days".

I hate goodbyes so this is definitely not a final goodbye, but more of a "See you later guys". I'll be back by November, once I'm all settled in life.

Until then, I wish you all the best of luck! Ever forward.

Langford
