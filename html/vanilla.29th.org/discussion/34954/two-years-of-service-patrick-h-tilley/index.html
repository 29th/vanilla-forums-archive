Two Years of Service (Patrick H. Tilley)

T3 Chandonnet
November 2019 in Promotions and Awards

Cpl. Nguyen said:

Patrick Henry Tilley, where do I start? This guy has been my brother-in-arms ever since we were put in the same 943rd TP 2 whole years ago. Of course, Cpl. Tilley ain't a DP2S2 seasoned-veteran like I am since he was assigned first to DP2S1 for an entirety of 6 days. We weren't close at first but one day he said something that sticks to my mind ever since, that one day we would be leading a squad together. I didn't believe it at the time since I used to doubt my abilities but I'm so glad that it became the reality. Through two years, Tilley has been a stellar private and PFC, serving S2 with his MG like a true Animal Mother. He has been continually putting great efforts into recruiting which I cannot stress enough how important it is. His endeavor with SLT landed him a promotion to Corporal and conveniently, gave me an ASL I can always trust. More important recently is his admirable efforts along with PFC Buckeye to help our squad perform our best in the Company's first tournament. It has truly been a great adventure with you for the past two years and I hope that no matter what comes next, your future with the unit will be as bright as ever and our friendship will not be hindered because we are, after all, brothers-in-arms. Congratulations for the two-year mark and the Rainbow my friend.
