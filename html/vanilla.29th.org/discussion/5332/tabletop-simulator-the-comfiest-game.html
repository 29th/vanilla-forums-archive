Tabletop Simulator - The comfiest game

T4 Andersson [Ret.]
July 2015 edited July 2015 in Other Games

Does anyone here play tabletop simulator? If you do not, but enjoy tabletop games, you should definitely look into it. Almost all great tabletop games such as Blood Bowl, Arkham Horror, Relic, Memoir 44 etc have been added as workshop mods. It is also great for roleplaying games and just having a good time fooling around, and you can usually get it for under ten bucks on a sale (there is one on GMG right now). Sounds comfy, right? Good way to relax after a particularly jarring battle!

It's pretty great if you like to relax with board games once in a while, only problem is finding serious players that are fun to chat with. Shouldn't be a problem in the 29th. Hit me up if you want to play sometime!
