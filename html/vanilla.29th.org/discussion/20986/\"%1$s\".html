Badge (Denis P. Ryasonen)

PFC Bye [Ret.]
October 2017 in Promotions and Awards

Sgt. Miochevich said:

Pvt. Ryasonen, despite being in our unit for relatively short time, has already distinguished himself among the ranks of the enlisted men. He is reliable and follows orders to the best of his abilities, and is a remarkably good shot. To my surprise, his skills have not yet been sufficiently recognised and rewarded, and I think it's nigh time that that is changed. It is thus that I am proud to present Pvt. Ryasonen with his Rifle Marksman Badge! Congratulations, Private!
