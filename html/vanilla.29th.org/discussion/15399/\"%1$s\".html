Seven Years of Service (Jason J. Hill)

T4 Phariss [Ret.]
November 2016 in Promotions and Awards

Fsgt. Balzer said:

Chief Warrant Officer 3rd Class Jason J. Hill is not that important. I mean, in 7 years of membership in the 29th he has only won one Silver Star. Sure he updates the 29th ArmA 3 servers, builds all the training missions, leads a Company and keeps contacts in the ArmA community. So what if every time there is a major ArmA or Mod update he needs to rebuild the training missions. So what if he spends countless hours looking for new and better ways to keep Charlie engaged and fighting. Does it matter he updates and creates content for the 29th wiki, polices the forums and puts in many hours behind the scenes? Who cares if he leads the rabble that is Charlie.. Seriously, what the hell would we do with out him. 14 AOCCS and 7 years, thank you Chief.
