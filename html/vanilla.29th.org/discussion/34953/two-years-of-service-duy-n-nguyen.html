Two Years of Service (Duy N. Nguyen)

T3 Chandonnet
November 2019 in Promotions and Awards

Cpl. Tilley said:

On November 12th, 2017, I opened Rising Storm 2 under my 29th recruit tags for the first time ever. It was my first day of BCT, and I logged in excited as can be, ready to see what sort of challenges and adventures lay ahead. At the end of the five days, while waiting to hear of the test results, I looked up and down the line of recruits, wondering who I would one day be leading others alongside. Apparently, the answer was nobody. Cpl. Nguyen did not show up for the final day of BCT. What a bum. He then proceeded to foil my plans of being the stand-out rookie of DP2S2 by blowing away leadership in drills, forcing me to live in his shadow. After only a few months, he was awarded a Good Conduct Medal for "being a pleasure to be around" ahhhh phooey! A few months go by, and I hear of then PFC Nguyen's placement in SLT. Ugh! That was supposed to be me! Nevertheless, two months later I was on his heels. Being the natural born leader that he is, Cpl. Nguyen chewed up and spit out SLT, completing the program and getting promoted to the rank of Cpl. within 3 months and immediately taking over DP2S2 as his own. Around the same time, DP2 participated in her first scrim, in which Cpl. Nguyen's actions garnered him a Silver Star and a Soldier's Medal, on top of the mere "participation trophies" that I received. It's all downhill from there. All jokes aside, I could not ask for a better battle buddy than the mean green fighting machine that I've learned to call my own during this wonderful 2 year stay in the 29th ID. The day I was placed in SLT, I told him, "One day, we will lead a squad together.” Look where we are now! A true role model to myself and others around him, he inspired me to chase excellence in pursuit of what I wanted to accomplish and has always set the bar high for those looking to achieve the same. Thanks for all the memories, and here's to the future! Oh yeah, and I'll always be one day older.
