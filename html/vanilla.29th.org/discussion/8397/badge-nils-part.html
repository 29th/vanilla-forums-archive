Badge (Nils Part)

T3 Lauritsen
December 2015 in Promotions and Awards

I finished PFC Part's CAB, 3rd award with the following statement "I am sure it will not be long before he will be getting his next combat action badge." Well PFC Part did not disappoint, he recruited 10 of the 24 in the 679th TP and threw in a couple more in other TPs. It was an impressive 30 days of recruiting done by PFC Part and it is my great pleasure to present him with his Combat Action Badge, 4th award. Thank you for your hard work and dedication to the 29th.
