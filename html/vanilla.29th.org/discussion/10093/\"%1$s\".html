BLUFOR WARNOs

FSgt. Balzer [Ret.]
February 2016 edited May 2016 in 29th ID A3 Charlie Operations
NATO CENTCOM
2nd MRB
WARNO
Operation Exodus Angel 		 

WARNO


1). Situation:

Information recovered from the downed UAV during Operation Elementary Recovery have confirmed intelligence gathered from multiple sources that iNation forces have acquired a viable nuclear device. The unstable nature and unclear goals of iNation have forced NATO to begin evacuating of near by Elektrozavodsk. A NATO led fleet has provided security for the civilian liner fleet. The Airforces of both sides have been fairly inactive due to the massive anti-aircraft capabilities of both the NATO fleet and the inland iNation forces. iNation forces have made it clear that they intend to stop the "illegal" excavation.

2). Mission:

The 2nd MRB, with elements of Bravo Company are tasked with halting iNation Armored forces from moving south from Mogilevka towards Elektrozavodsk. Enemy armor has been spotted being prepped just south west of Mogilevka. An attack is expected in the next few days. Slow or stop the enemy armor attack. A local militia Checkpoint has been spotted near 098,107. The militia force has placed anti tank mines on the roadway on or near the Checkpoint. Local militia forces are known to be hostile to both iNation and NATO forces. The 2nd MRB is tasked with moving to, capturing and holding the checkpoint. Those mines should halt or slow the enemy armored advance. It is also expected iNation forces will be moving south to clear a path for their armor. Defend the mines at check point. Also, Friendly forces are preparing anti armor positions near your kick off point. Defend these positions.

  • 1). Take and hold the Checkpoint at 098,107
  • 2). Defend anti armor assets at or near your start zone
  • 3). Destroy enemy armor assets near 082,104


3). General Instructions.

A). Organization/Command:

2nd MRB in over all command of Bluefor

. Weapons and Equipment:

Unlimited 5.56 faction specific rifles with standard optics (ACOG/RCO or less)
2X faction correct MGs
2X Medics
1X Faction correct Single Shot AT launcher (AT-4)
1X Faction correct Multi Launcher 4 rounds total
2X GLs - 3 rd GLs are banned
2X 7.62 Rifle with standard optics (ACOG/RCO or less)
1X Sniper/marksman

  • No Thermals - None, no gun optics with thermals or binoc thermals.
  • 2 Silencers for Bluefor.
  • No guided Launchers.
  • Range finders with night vision for Squad Leaders and above ONLY.
  • All others may have binocs.
  • NVGs are allowed.
  • Satchel Explosives are allowed (6 total).
  • Reasonable and realistic kit, ammo, loadouts,etc.

D). Intelligence:

Hostile Forces:

Local Militia Forces are known to be hostile to both iNation and NATO forces. They blame both for the conflict in Chenarus. Made up of retired Armed forces of Chenarus members, local police and other paramilitary groups, they are assumed to be small in numbers and loosely organized. Lightly armed with Soviet era equipment, this small force should be considered competent and motivated.

iNation forces recently encountered were Charlovi Battalion. This formerly Bystican formation is now aligned with iNation and is equipped with modern Eastern and Western equipment.

Dammage to Civilian personnel, equipment and property is to be avoided at all costs.
Weapons free on iNation and militia forces.

    1). Situation:

    Intelligence has solid information that iNation forces intend on deploying foot mobile forces to cut communications from Stratis to the mainland. The Stratis Air Base has limited defenses, and could be the target for further enemy action. The loss of the towers could mean the loss of the air base. With the current communication issues, the loss of this air base would be a major blow to NATO air power in the area. There are 5 towers of key importance, they are detailed below. These coms towers are important for Stratis to maintain solid communication with the outside world.

    2). Mission:

    Primary mission is to defend (5) communication towers #1-#5. Towers 1, 2 & 3 are Satellite link capable, while 4 & 5 are short range microwave sets. iNation forces are likely to be inserted on the South of Stratis, and advance on the coms towers. 42 Commando is to defend all 5 towers to the best of its abilities. Elimination of enemy forces are secondary to the Coms tower defense.

    3). General Instructions.

    42 Commando is to deploy to a ready station near the objective coms towers. 40 Commando is to deploy its forces in a manner to defend all the coms towers. Secure enemy radios, GPS and maps to gather intelligence.
    1) Defend the towers
    2) Eliminate iNation forces
    3) Secure Enemy Intel
