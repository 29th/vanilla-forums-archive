Promotion and Badges (Tony A. Stephens)

T3 Grimes [Ret.]
May 2019 in Promotions and Awards

Cpl. Gibson said:

Over the time I have known Pvt. Stephens, he has brought a smile to the members of everyone in EP3S4. One of the most charismatic soldiers I know and he always knows how to raise the morale of the squad. A man that's eager to fight I can always count on him to be by our side and carry his own weight. And when it hits the fan you can always hear a sly remark from a certain young man who raises everyone up from the dirt in the worse times in combat. We're very lucky to have him in our squad and being here for three months he has earned himself the promotion to PFC the AoCC and Rifle Marksman Badge. Congratulations PFC Stephens, on serving for three months and here's to another three months in the unit. Keep our hopes high in our rifles raised. Ever forward!

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of

Tony A. Stephens       138789385      EP3S4

I do promote him to Private, First Class in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 21st day of May 2019 .

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
