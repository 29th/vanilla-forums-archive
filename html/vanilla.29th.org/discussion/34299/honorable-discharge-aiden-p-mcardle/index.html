Honorable Discharge (Aiden P. McArdle)

T4 Ceglar [Ret.]
October 2019 in Discharges

HONORABLE DISCHARGE

FROM THE ARMED FORCES OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA

This is to certify that

Aiden P. McArdle     Cpl.     EP2S3

was Honorably Discharged from the

UNITED STATES ARMY

on the 2nd day of October 2019. This certificate is awarded

as a testimonial of Honest and Faithful Service





ARPC-OPM-O

DATE: 2nd October 2019

MEMORANDUM FOR Commander,

U.S. Army 29th Infantry Division, ATTN: AHRC-OP-S, 1 Reserve Way

Fort Belvoir, VA 22060


SUBJECT: Unqualified Resignation


1.I hereby tender my unqualified resignation as an Enlisted Soldier in the

United States Army under the provisions of Chapter 6, Section III,

AR 135-175.

2.I understand that if my resignation is accepted I am entitled to an

honorable separation and will be furnished an Honorable Discharge

Certificate. I understand that I cannot resign if I am a statutory obligor.


Signature
Aiden P. McArdle
Name (Type or Print)
Private, First Class Squad Leader Easy Company, Second Platoon, Third Squad
Rank Position Platoon/Squad

"I have been in this unit for more than a year, and I still don't know the exact reasons why I stayed. Maybe it was because of all the friends I made over these past months or the experience I had with all of you. The amount of interesting and fascinating people I have met while being here is crazy. My first day of BCT started off with TSgt. Dethfield (Sgt at the time) and SSgt. Robles yelling at me to get in line a join the rest of the recruits. Man how they have changed since then. I joined the line and the person standing next to me was Pvt. Mellinger. That same Pvt. became my closest friend in the unit and by that time I didn't know it. After BCT I was introduced to another one of my closest friends now which was PFC Sutera. Sometime after I started my journey into SLT as being the youngest in the unit to pursue it at the time. Soon after going into SLT I became the ASL of EP2S3. After some time I ended up being the SL of EP2S3 with Cpl. Leliveld. Me and Cpl. Leliveld focused on hammering out all of the squad's problems and turning it into the squad you see today. Id like to thank some people who have been great friends so here we go, Tsgt. Dethfield, SSgt. Robles (thank you for getting me into anime and I'm not a weeb), Sgt. Sutera, Cpl. Mellinger, PFC York, PFC Evarts (Get your T/5 already), PFC Filho, PFC Matsuzaka, and finally my boys in EP2S3. I will leave you guys off with a quote to remember me by "One never really knows how much one has been touched by a place until one has left it." - Thomas Jefferson."
Reason for leaving
