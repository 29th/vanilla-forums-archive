Honorable Discharge (Bill Deakin)

Maj. Cooke [Ret.]
July 2015 in Discharges


HONORABLE DISCHARGE

FROM THE ARMED FORCES OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA

This is to certify that

Bill Deakin    Sergeant    AP1HQ

was Honorably Discharged from the

UNITED STATES ARMY

on the 12th day of JULY 2015. This certificate is awarded

as a testimonial of Honest and Faithful Service





ARPC-OPM-O

DATE: 12 JUL 2015

MEMORANDUM FOR Commander,

U.S. Army 29th Infantry Division, ATTN: AHRC-OP-S, 1 Reserve Way

Fort Belvoir, VA 22060


SUBJECT: Unqualified Resignation


1.I hereby tender my unqualified resignation as an Enlisted Soldier in the

United States Army under the provisions of Chapter 6, Section III,

AR 135-175.

2.I understand that if my resignation is accepted I am entitled to an

honorable separation and will be furnished an Honorable Discharge

Certificate. I understand that I cannot resign if I am a statutory obligor.


Bill Deakin
Signature
Bill Deakin
Name (Type or Print)
Sergeant Platoon Sergeant Able Company, First Platoon
Rank Position Platoon/Squad
From Sgt. Deakin:

After over 4 years in the 29th the time has come for me to step down. As some will have noticed, my attendance has been in rapid decline recently which thanks to issues in real life won't be improving any time soon. I wont forget my time or the friends I've made here and I'm glad to have been given the chance to reach as high as I have in regards to leadership. However, AP1 needs someone who they can consistently rely on to be there to lead them and currently that isn't me. I wish them and the rest of the 29th the best and I know they will continue to push on and impress.
Reason for leaving
