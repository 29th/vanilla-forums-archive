Two Years and Six Months of Service (Jonas J. Gates)

T5 Kasałka
June 2020 in Promotions and Awards

PFC Tan said:

PFC Gates joined us a few weeks ago, immediately showing his new comrades in the platoon that he is an experienced and veteran soldier, that should not be underestimated in combat. Just in his first week of reenlistment he reacted well when under fire and caused massive casualties on enemies in platoon drills. He came back to us just in time for me to award him his 5th Army of Occupation Medal, which marks 2 years and 6 months of service. Congratulations PFC Gates.
