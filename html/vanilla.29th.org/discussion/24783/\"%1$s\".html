Two Years of Service (Ezequiel M. Alan)

T5 Yukki
February 2018 in Promotions and Awards

Sgt. Peroni said:

I can't believe it's been two years already. Two years since I recruited PFC Alan into the 29th. In this lapse of time, the good PFC grew up immensely as a member, and it is safe to say that he his one of the most stable members I had the pleasure of spending drills with. Whether it is while destroying enemies with his MG, or just when hanging out on TS, PFC Alan is always in a good mood. He always goes the extra mile, both at drills and with his Enlistment Liaison job, which he's been doing for a long time now. Long story short, PFC Alan is the perfect example of how rewarding and beneficial recruiting can be within the 29th. I am happy that I bumped into you back then. For his service in our unit, PFC Alan is being awarded his 4th AoCC as well as his World War I Victory Medal. Congratulations, PFC! Stay with us for much more.
