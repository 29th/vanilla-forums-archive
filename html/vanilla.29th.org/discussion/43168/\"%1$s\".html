Badge (Christopher M. Cowles)

Technician, 4th Grade Adam K. Stachurski
February 2021 in Promotions and Awards

2Lt. Deb said:

There is a lot I can say about Private, First Class Christopher M. Cowles. Back when PFC Cowles joined the 29th and was only but a child in terms of the 29th, it became apparent that Lighthouse would be his bread and butter. He very quickly joined as a Recruitment Secretary and followed that up as an Enlistment Clerk. He would do his work until eventually being promoted to Private, First Class where his recruitment efforts would take off drastically. Within two and a half months, PFC Cowles would earn all four CABs, but this was not all of it. He would continue recruiting multiple members a week and grow our training platoons to the point where we required more training staff, and that’s where PFC Cowles stepped in. Already being a Recruitment Secretary and Enlistment Clerk, he would apply and become an Assistant Drill Instructor. These countless recruits that have been brought into the unit have made the difference, and him going out of his way to train them makes it all the better. In recent times, PFC Cowles has become the Senior Enlistment Clerk because of his hasty workflow and irreplaceable attitude. He has brought over one hundred people to this unit who have attended multiple days of BCT, and this does not account for all the people who did not enlist. PFC Cowles has gone out of his way to communicate to hundreds of people and his efforts are not in vain. For your efforts, I am proud to award you your Recruiters Badge. Congratulations, PFC Cowles, and please continue recruiting so that I can see your name at the top of the leaderboards.
