Award (Able Company)

T3 Lauritsen
September 2016 edited September 2016 in Promotions and Awards

1Lt. Fritz said:

Not too long ago, Able Company created a small Expeditionary Force to go out into the RO2 Community and battled a German supported French Panzer Grenadier unit. This action, although not an official deployment, still carried with it the reputation of the unit as a whole. The men of Able Company, thirsty for action, volunteered in droves to be a part of this special event. In less than a week, the leader of the expedition, WO1 Lagenbrunner of AP2 studied the map till he knew it like the back of his hand. With rushed training, the men of Able who came from all over the Company formed a comprehensive fighting force that engaged and effectively destroyed the enemy force. Because of their valor, dedication, and honed abilities, Company HQ is awarding these members their Army Forces Expeditionary Medal for participation in a sanctioned unofficial scrimmage and a job well done. Wear it with pride!
