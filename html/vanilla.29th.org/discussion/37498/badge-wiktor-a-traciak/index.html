Badge (Wiktor A. Traciak)

T5 Vulpen
May 2020 in Promotions and Awards

PFC Faletny said:

PFC Traciak has been a crewman for over a year now. Having earned his Armor AQB, he set his eyes on the other award - Pilot AQB. He started as a competent pilot, with occasional hiccups here and there as part of the learning process. During this process, not only did he hone his skills to a much higher level, but he also helped train other crewmen in CP2. His knowledge of vehicles and weapons exceeds that of many, given his time spent on figuring out what works best. He even went as far as reporting vehicle-related bugs with other crewmen. His accuracy and tenacity in ground support are out-right terrifying, to say the least.

It should come as no surprise, that this man's actions have earned him a reasonable award. PFC Traciak, I want to award you your Pilot Marksman Badge. Now that you have wings, don't fly too close to the sun.
