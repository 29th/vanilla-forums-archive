Four Years and Six Months of Service (Nicholas J. Phariss)

2Lt. Bergstrom
August 2016 edited August 2016 in Promotions and Awards

Cpl. Warner said:

If you cut a fish in half can you count its rings to determine its age? Scientists have wondered this for millennia and may wonder still for millennia yet to come. All I know is that if you split the Dirty First's very own fish in half you may just find him to be sporting 4 and a half of the most beautiful Blue and Grey rings you have ever seen. With this infallible logic, T/4 Phariss has earned his 9th Army of Occupation medal. May your rings continue to grow, T/4.
