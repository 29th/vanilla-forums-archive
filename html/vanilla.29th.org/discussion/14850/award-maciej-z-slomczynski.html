Award (Maciej Z. Slomczynski)

Cpt. Patty
October 2016 edited October 2016 in Promotions and Awards

Cpt. Patty said:

Technician, 3rd Grade Maciej Z. Slomczynski , a name that I have tried to spell many a time only to fail horribly. Otherwise more simply known as T3 Swomma, he was generously applauded for his fine work in creating the forum.

However he is not just a good hand with the forum, he is also the Company Clerk of Baker, and this is a role he equally excels at. Not only is his spelling much better than mine he has also been carrying out his clerking duties above and beyond of what anyone could expect of any 29th'er.

Never missing a beat he ensures the clerking needs of the Company are not just merely met, but handled with the extreme efficiency, speed and highest standards we have come to expect from him. Indeed one could say he was born to be the clerk of Baker Company, as he does such a fine job none of us can imagine Company life without him.

For his outstanding services to clerking I am happy to award him the good conduct medal.
