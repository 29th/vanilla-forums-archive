Four Years and Six Months of Service (Christopher D. Ochs)

T3 Chandonnet
September 2019 in Promotions and Awards

SSgt. Wimpfheimer said:

2Lt. Ochs is an exemplary member of the 29th. His financial contributions to the unit are without equal as well as his dedication to duty and steadfastness. He has become DP2's officer and currently sits as Chief Finance Officer for the unit. He has recently hit his 4 year and 6 month mark in the 29th awarding him his 9th AOCC. Personally, it has been an absolute pleasure to serve under his command. Congratulations, 2Lt. Ochs on your 9th AOCC.
