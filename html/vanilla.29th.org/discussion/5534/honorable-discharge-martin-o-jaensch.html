Honorable Discharge (Martin O. Jänsch)

Cpt. Dashwood [Ret.]
July 2015 in Discharges


HONORABLE DISCHARGE

FROM THE ARMED FORCES OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA

This is to certify that


Martin O. Jänsch    Corporal    Squad Leader

was Honorably Discharged from the

UNITED STATES ARMY

on the 28th day of July 2015. This certificate is awarded

as a testimonial of Honest and Faithful Service





ARPC-OPM-O

DATE: 28 JUL 2015

MEMORANDUM FOR Commander,

U.S. Army 29th Infantry Division, ATTN: AHRC-OP-S, 1 Reserve Way

Fort Belvoir, VA 22060


SUBJECT: Unqualified Resignation


1.I hereby tender my unqualified resignation as an Enlisted Soldier in the

United States Army under the provisions of Chapter 6, Section III,

AR 135-175.

2.I understand that if my resignation is accepted I am entitled to an

honorable separation and will be furnished an Honorable Discharge

Certificate. I understand that I cannot resign if I am a statutory obligor.


Martin O. Jänsch
Signature
MARTIN O. JÄNSCH
Name (Type or Print)
Corporal Squad Leader Baker Company, First Platoon, Second Squad
Rank Position Platoon/Squad
From Cpl. Jänsch:

It's been fantastic 5 years. I met some extraordinary people and found friends, had a lot of fun and learned quite a bit. Starting as a simple Rifleman like everyone, getting my hands on different AITs and mastering them, leading an office at some point and now beeing even a Squadleader .. this variety, coupled with this professionalism is what i think can not be found anywhere else in a online community. And i wouldn't want to miss a single day of all this.
Being in this great unit actually helped me a lot, building confidence and teaching me how to deal with some of my biggest personal issues, but as it is right now i can not be an effective and productive Squadleader AND deal with the remaining problems i have.
This is no final good bye.
I'll be back
Corporal Martin O. Jänsch
