A Tribute to Captain Fritz

PFC Bull
September 2020 edited September 2020 in Public Discussion

Gentlemen, some of you have heard of the fascination I have with history, and in keeping with my fascination, I decided to create some dress uniform sets of members within the Blue and Gray.


FC Link

LB Link

HVI Link

SP Link

NT Link

HVF Link

First on the list is Captain Fritz, due to the original WWII uniform set I acquired having original WWII sewn-in bullion Captains' ranks, (which I would never butcher), it was fairly clear who I would "choose" first. Captain Fritz, your uniform was a joy to create. I learned many things about the rarities of each award, and how this unit conducts its uniform versus the real life counterpart. I will not bore you gentlemen with the details in this post, but rest assured, if you would ever let me talk your ears off in the TeamSpeak about it, lord knows I would. Thank you for letting me put together this uniform for you sir, as I said, it was a pleasure.

(If anyone would want me to create their uniform, ribbon rack, or shadow box, or even nominate someone for their uniform creation, please feel free to contact me. Rah and Kill!)
