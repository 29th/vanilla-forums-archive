Promotion (Kelly J. Fritz) 

T3 Grimes [Ret.]
July 2020 in Promotions and Awards

FSgt. Dethfield said:

1Lt. Fritz may not have been in Easy since its formation, but since joining Easy company a year and a half ago, he has been a driving force for the company. The great amount of activity, events, scrims, and other exciting times in the company have been largely pushed by him. It is not always easy dealing with the troubles of running a company as large as Easy, but he keeps everyone grounded and focused while also keeping the spirits of the men up. He can be regularly seen teasing the soldiers in formation, making jokes, and talking about the latest gossip in Hollywood before company drills begin. I couldn't ask for a better CO, and although he is an insufferable Prussiaboo, its something I'm willing to put up with. Congratulations on the promotion to Captain.

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of

Kelly J. Fritz       047232271      Easy Co. HQ

I do promote him to Captain in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 12th day of July 2020 .

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
