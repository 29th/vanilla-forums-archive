Award (Teemu J. Tuomainen)

Cpl. Browning [Ret.]
April 2015 edited April 2015 in Promotions and Awards

Half way through a tough defence for the Allied team, T5 Tuomainen noticed that things had gone quiet on his flank. Suspecting that there might be an opportunity to take advantage of the situation, he communicated with his team to find out where the enemy might be. Upon hearing that the enemy where not to be seen on his flank, T5 Tuomainen took the initiative and asked his team leader for permission to push into the enemy's rear. Permission was granted and T5 Tuomainen made his move.

As the T5 advanced into ground previously held by the opposition, he kept in constant communication with his squad so that he was sure he would not be mistaken for the enemy and shot upon and so that his team could direct him to the enemy.
After careful manoeuvring, T5 Tuomainen found his first enemy and dispatched him. Continuing his cautious advance behind enemy lines, T5 Tuomainen found his second target with help from his squad.
By this time the enemy had become aware of T5 Tuomainen presence and began to make efforts to secure their rear and hunt him down. However, the T5 was patient and did not expose him self with careless rushes, choosing instead to play carefully and stay hidden when he could, using the intel from his squad to navigate around and behind the enemy. At times he was less than a dozen feet from the enemy who he distracted by throwing defensive grenades before moving to remain hidden.
Fining another of the enemy, he asked his squad to fire towards him in order to distract the German. It worked and T5 Tuomainen was able to kill the distracted enemy from behind.
The enemy team was now down to their final man and the Allied team went in to hunt him down. Hearing footsteps, T5 Tuomainen came out of cover and killed the final enemy to win the round.

I am happy to Award T5 Tuomainen the Bronze Star ! Congratulations T5!
