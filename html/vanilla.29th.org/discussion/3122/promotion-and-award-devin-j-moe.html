Promotion and Award (Devin J. Moe)

T4 Phariss [Ret.]
April 2015 edited April 2015 in Promotions and Awards

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of


Devin J. Moe      76561198050233590      CP2S1

I do promote him to CORPORAL in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 19th day of APRIL 2015.

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.




SSgt. Bergstrom said:
It’s been a long road, a hard road and a hot road. As we all know during road trips things can get irritable, your seat mate won’t always stay on their side and it seems like you always have to pee right after the last rest stop fades into the rearview mirror. You have stayed on that road and you’ve driven hard. You’ve listened to the wizened navigators who’ve driven this road before and you learned where the potholes and speed traps were. You showed that you can handle yourself and a team and you have demonstrated a skill that we in the 29th value greatly - leadership. May your roads be smooth and your radio be satellite but having graduated this program we know you’ll do fine with a dirt road and an 8-track. Salute
