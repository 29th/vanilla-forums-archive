Honourable Discharge (Benjamin D. Donofrio)

Cpt. Hill [Ret.]
May 2018 edited May 2018 in Discharges

HONORABLE DISCHARGE

FROM THE ARMED FORCES OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA

This is to certify that

Benjamin D. Donofrio     Corporal     CP2S1

was Honorably Discharged from the

UNITED STATES ARMY

on the 26th day of May 2018. This certificate is awarded

as a testimonial of Honest and Faithful Service





ARPC-OPM-O

DATE: 26 MAY 2018

MEMORANDUM FOR Commander,

U.S. Army 29th Infantry Division, ATTN: AHRC-OP-S, 1 Reserve Way

Fort Belvoir, VA 22060


SUBJECT: Unqualified Resignation


  1. I hereby tender my unqualified resignation as an Enlisted Soldier in the
    United States Army under the provisions of Chapter 6, Section III, AR 135-175.

  2. I understand that if my resignation is accepted I am entitled to an
    honorable separation and will be furnished an Honorable Discharge
    Certificate. I understand that I cannot resign if I am a statutory obligor.


Signature
Benjamin D. Donofrio
Name (Type or Print)
Corporal Squad Leader Charlie Company, Second Platoon, First Squad
Rank Position Platoon/Squad
Cpl. Donofrio said:
Due to the personal agenda I have at home I would like to step down as SL of S1, and request a discharge from the 29th. I've recently graduated college, and just need the extra time to right now to make sure I have my career goals set, and can get settled into a proper job, without time constraints.

When things settle down in the distant future with me it is a goal of mine to return. It's been a pleasure to serve S1, and the 29th.

Best regards,
Benjamin Donofrio
