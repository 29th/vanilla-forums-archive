Eight Years and Six Months of Service (Glenn S. Hart)

CW4 Slomczynski
May 2015 in Promotions and Awards

Cpt. Walker said:

Msgt. Hart has made it into the records with his now 17th AOCC award. This means that this veteran has provide his time, knowledge and will to making the 29th happen for a total of eight years and six months. This is a feat that very few have made. It is rather hard to image such a thing happening. Most of us old timers in the 29th thought that making four years was extraordinary, but the MSgt. has doubled this. Please, when you see the MSgt. in TeamSpeak, send him a poke or a message thanking him for his time in. Even if you see this post and post in thread, take the extra effort and tell him thanks. He has done a hell of a lot for the unit and deserve a good pat on the back for his great times and experiences.
