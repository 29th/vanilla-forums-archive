Badge (Jakub J. Kasałka)

T5 Vulpen
April 2020 edited April 2020 in Promotions and Awards

2Lt. Stray said:

PFC Kasalka took to crewman with little experience but what he lacked in experience he made up for with the dedication he had in bettering his pilot skills. Over the months I witnessed him practising in any spare moment he had available on top of constantly asking for tips on how he could improve. In the moments that he lost his air asset to pilot error or through the conditions of the battlefield, he bore the weight of the responsibility, understood how to do better next time and I could see that he was annoyed with the loss. This is the sign of a dedicated crewman who strives to become better and that’s all we ask for here in the 29th.

I find it fitting that PFC Kasalka is awarded his Pilot Marksman badge, congratulations PFC.
