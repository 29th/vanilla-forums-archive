Honorable Discharge (Marcus V. Zim)

T4 Burg
April 2020 in Discharges

HONORABLE DISCHARGE

FROM THE ARMED FORCES OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA

This is to certify that

Marcus V. Zim     T/5     CP1S3

was Honorably Discharged from the

UNITED STATES ARMY

on the 7th day of April 2020. This certificate is awarded

as a testimonial of Honest and Faithful Service





ARPC-OPM-O

DATE: 7 April 2020

MEMORANDUM FOR Commander,

U.S. Army 29th Infantry Division, ATTN: AHRC-OP-S, 1 Reserve Way

Fort Belvoir, VA 22060


SUBJECT: Unqualified Resignation


1.I hereby tender my unqualified resignation as an Enlisted Soldier in the

United States Army under the provisions of Chapter 6, Section III,

AR 135-175.

2.I understand that if my resignation is accepted I am entitled to an

honorable separation and will be furnished an Honorable Discharge

Certificate. I understand that I cannot resign if I am a statutory obligor.


Marcus V. Zim
Signature
Marcus V. Zim
Name (Type or Print)
Technician, 5th Grade Rifleman Charlie Company, First Platoon, Third Squad
Rank Position Platoon/Squad
“ This is something that ive been delaying for quite some time now, but its time to face the reality and make it official, im requesting this discharge after a long reflection and I decided that its time to go. In this past year ive been struggling to find motivation to keep going, even by showing for 2 hours a week was hard, and I was being unfair with leadership by having bad attendance when they try very hard to bring decent drills to all of us. I had a great time the last 8 years and 8 months, alot of fun moments, drills, events and random gaming moments. I made alot of good friends in the unit that will last for life. So id like with this to say goodbye for the time being, ill still be around sometimes for some drills or open events. Id also like to send my best wishes and good luck for everyone in the unit going forward.”
Reason for leaving
APPROVED
Approved/Disapproved
