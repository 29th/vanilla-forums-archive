Promotion (Christopher D. Ochs)

Technician, 4th Grade Adam K. Stachurski
November 2020 in Promotions and Awards

FSgt. Cyr said:

It's been almost 2 years since Dog Company had a Company Officer at the helm. Originally when the Company was founded Cpt. Fritz was at the helm (after divorcing me in Able Company and leaving me alone for about a year). However Cpt. Fritz eventually had to retire for a time and this saw the ascension of 1Lt. Derbyshire as the Officer for Dog Company. When we closed Able Company and I came to Dog I finally had an officer again but unfortunately 1Lt. Derbyshire was cold and distant (probably not happy that I wiggled my way into our relationship and was very needy). 1Lt. however had to retire as well from Dog Company for a while (I assume due to tea addiction and tea rehab, you know those brits) and once again Dog and myself were without a Company Officer.

During all this we had a 2Lt. who was there all the time, making a name for himself and his platoon and laid groundwork for our 3rd platoon expansion by training up some surefire leadership. However his platoon needed him for a time and we were not ready to produce more officers to take up reigns in the open positions. With time and work we've gotten to a point where we are projected to have officers present in each platoon and we felt time to move this man up. This man you ask? 2Lt. Ochs. Having pulled this man up from DP2 HQ to now run the show as Dog Company Commanding Officer something didn't feel right. It was not the man however but the rank. Battalion HQ and myself have decided that for him to truly reign as our Glorious Officer he'll need a rank and title befitting of his new found position. Therefore Battalion HQ as well as myself are hereby promoting him to the rank of 1st Lieutenant and the title of FSgt. Cyr's Sugar Daddy (Wait, we're not giving him that title? Oh... disappointing.) Congratulations, 1Lt., excited to have you here!

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of

Christopher D. Ochs       039478171       Dog Company HQ

I do promote him to First Lieutenant in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 29TH day of November 2020 .

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
