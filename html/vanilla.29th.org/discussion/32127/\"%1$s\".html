Promotion and Badges (Max K. Halbieren)

T3 Grimes [Ret.]
June 2019 in Promotions and Awards

Cpl. Crane said:

Halburen. No, that's not right, Halbeeeren. No, still not right. For those who know Private Halbieren, you know the issue of listening to the numerous ways to pronounce his name. While the rest of us are trying to pronounce Private Halbieren's name correctly the fine Private is busy killing baddies, taking names, and obliterating entire buildings by himself. Despite the big red cross usually on him from his obsession with being the squad's medic it seems he more often takes life than he saves it, though there is certainly no small amount of medic'ing that he does. To his squad he is a trusted and beloved comrade and to his foes he is an enemy worthy of fear, reminiscent of Bat Man or another terrifying monster with a difficult name to pronounce. These awards and the promotion to PFC seems the only way to calm his hunger! Congratulations, PFC "Haalbuuron", and keep up the good work!

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of

Max K. Halbieren       046702514      EP3S1

I do promote him to Private, First Class in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 4th day of June 2019 .

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
