Four Years of Service (David J. Campbell)

CW4 Slomczynski
February 2016 edited February 2016 in Promotions and Awards

Maj. Cooke said:

1Lt. Campbell has now been in the 29th ID for over four years! During this time, he has been an incredible asset to the 29th, a mentor to many leaders. His work as Battalion S-1 may not always be the most visible, but believe me when I say that the 29th would definitely know it if he wasn't making it look so easy. 1Lt. Campbell: the 29th thanks you for everything you've done and your commitment to the 29th ID for the last four years! I look forward to seeing what you accomplish over the next four!
