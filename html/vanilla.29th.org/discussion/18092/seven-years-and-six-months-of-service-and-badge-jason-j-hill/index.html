Seven Years and Six Months of Service and Badge (Jason J. Hill)

T4 Burg
May 2017 edited May 2017 in Promotions and Awards

FSgt. Balzer said:

Seven years and Six months... Going on Seven Years Seven Months. Sorta like a Seven and Seven. You know, a cocktail of Seagrams 7 Canadian Whiskey and 7Up. He is a Sweet and smokey mix of... Wait. Skip the Sweet. Maybe 50:1 Seven to Seven? Chief Warrant Officer Hill is the backbone of Charlie. He creates our training missions, sets our policies and guides us both on and off the field. This poor guy has been putting up with us and our kind for Seven Years and Six months. Chief has never stopped pushing to improve the 29th's place in the ArmA community... Wait again, skip the improve. Chief has created the 29th's place in the ArmA community and guided Charlie through many ups and downs.

Maybe Chief is like that bottle of Canadian Whiskey. He may be strong at times, but his quality has guided us through tough times. He is that blend of qualities that makes for the perfect moment enjoyed with company. Charlie Company. And if that does not wet your whistle, Chief has taught Charlie can beat you with the bottle.
I also have no idea how chief has avoided his Marksman Arms Qualification as Automatic Rifleman. This over site has been corrected.
