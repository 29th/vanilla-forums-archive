Badge (Nathan de Crom)

Cpl. Saerens [Ret.]
July 2016 in Promotions and Awards

Sgt. Novak said:

PFC de Crom is kinda newcomer in squad 1, he has been here for a couple of weeks only, but it doesn't mean we've never heard about him before. Mostly every person, being asked about their opinion, can easily characterize him not only as a great fellow but also, what in some way is even more important, as an outstanding marksman. The sight of PFC de Crom with his rifle stock against his shoulder can stop the beat of enemy heart seconds before he actually kills him. I had a great pleasure to recently observe him during the drills and must admit that his rifle skill doesn't add up with only Marksman Badge, and in my opinion, its time for one more milestone in his progress. With all that said, congratulations with your rifle Sharpshooter badge, PFC!
