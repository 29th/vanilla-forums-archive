Four Years of Service (Buck Barclay)

Technician, 4th Grade Adam K. Stachurski
September 2018 in Promotions and Awards

SSgt. Novak said:

Sometimes it seems to be ages ago i met 2Lt. Barclay for the first time. It hit me real quick that this person, coming over as a Platoon leader could drastically change the overall course of things using his experience and qualification. Much time has passed since that moment and i never felt myself upset of such a assignment nor i had any kind of side thoughts about it. 2Lt. Barclay was and remains a true example of professionalism being a person you can always rely on, no matter what's going on around, how hard the situation is and etc.. It's been countless times we were discussing some things related to the Platoon and just important ones in general and i really figured out a lot for me. Can't be too late to learn something new, after all. It came to my notice that he's been around here for rather a round date, precisely 4 years, which makes me eligible to award him his 8th AOCC! Congrats Sir!
