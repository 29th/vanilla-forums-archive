Seven Years of Service (Thimo R. Friesenhan)

T4 Orhan
October 2016 in Promotions and Awards

Sgt. Ternby said:

Here we have a very well known member of the unit, T/5 Friesenhan. I think most people in Baker know the reason for why he is so well known and feared in game, due to them experiencing it first-hand. T/5 Friesenhan's skill in DH is pretty much unrivaled. Give him a weapon, ANY weapon, and he'll more often than not end up with a whole bunch of kills. It isn't co-incidental, however, that T/5 Friesenhan is as skilled as he is. He's spent countless hours (6290 to be exact) in-game, a lot of that time pubbing, in order to practice his in-game skill and get to the skill level that he is at right now. He's also one of very few 29th'ers in the unit's history, to have gotten an expert badge with a weapon, and I can't think of anyone more deserving than him. Having him in my squad is a real pleasure and I couldn't picture the squad without him. For having been in the unit for a total of SEVEN years, T/5 Friesenhan is now eligible for his 14th AOCC Medal. Congratulations T/5!!! Hope to see even more of these in the future!
