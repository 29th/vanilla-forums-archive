Promotion and Badge (Olaf A. Alder)

T4 Burg
February 2017 edited February 2017 in Promotions and Awards

Cpl. van Gastel said:

Look who we have here. Pvt. Alder who has gotten his 1st AOCC a few weeks ago! Looks like he's ready for the next level within Charlie Company as this soldier has done a remarkable job the time he performed in Squad 1 as the combat medic in need. Heard him time after time saying off- and on the field that he enjoys being there to help everyone out when they run out of medical gear but nonetheless, Pvt. Alder has been showing some really fine work with aiming and working his way around the working within the platoon. As this soldier is no longer a new private and has shown good awareness of his surroundings and marksmanship with his rifle, I'm delighted to award him with his EIB and promote him to the rank of PFC! Outstanding, PFC Alder!

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of

Olaf A. Alder      068060804      CP2S1

I do promote him to Private, First Class in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 12th day of February 2017 .

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
