Promotion (Elliott L. Patterson)

Cpt. Dashwood [Ret.]
July 2015 in Promotions and Awards

"I will give to the selfless performance of my duty and my mission the best that effort, thought, and dedication can provide. To this end, I will not only seek continually to improve my knowledge and practice of my profession, but also I will exercise the authority entrusted to me by the President and the Congress with fairness, justice, patience, and restraint, respecting the dignity and human rights of others and devoting myself to the welfare of those place under my command."

Selfless, fair, just and patient. Able to not only make the best use of himself, but of his subordinates as well. To utilise those under him to the best of their ability and to drive them to the excellence they are worthy of attaining. Just few of the countless tasks an officer must excel in. It is not a job for everyone. Not everyone has the abilty, the drive or the peerless dedication to do it.

1Lt. Patterson is one of the few individuals who showed that not only was he perfectly able to do the job and get it done, but he was able to ensure that everything was ship-shape and squared away. Since 1Lt. Browning's transfer to Able Co. 1Lt. Patterson has taken over Baker Company and driven it to new glory. The company simply could not have survived had he not stepped up to take the reigns!

With endless enthusiasm and tireless motivation 1Lt. Patterson continues to set the standard for quality in HQ and for this Battalion HQ has seen fit to promote 1Lt. Patterson to the rank of Captain! Congratulations Sir!

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of


Elliott L. Patterson     12456985     Commanding Officer, Baker Company

I do promote him to Captain in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 08th day of July 2015.

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
