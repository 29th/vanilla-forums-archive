Promotion (Adam K. Stachurski)

Sgt. Maj Cyr
April 2018 in Promotions and Awards

Able's Company Clerk Stachurski has been clerking for nearly 6 months now, first as a wee Platoon Clerk and then as T/4 Borkowski's successor to the position of Able Company clerk. Not unfamiliar with the logistical side of working in the 29th as he's been an Enlistment Liaison for Lighthouse a bit longer. Nobly recording the events of Able's ventures through the 3 theater chaos that is the hybridized beast of RO2/RS with nothing but his pen and assortment of SMGs (supposedly they also fire pens). Rumor has it his Platoon HQ was going to promote him to T/5 before I stole him away as my Company clerk. Hah! Now I get to beat them to the punch. Without further ado it is hereby my pleasure to officially post that PFC Stachurski is no longer and is a properly minted Technician Fifth Grade. Congratulations T/5, keep up the good work!

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of


Adam K. Stachurski     105036382     AP1S3

I do promote him to Technician Fifth Grade in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 22TH day of APRIL 2018.

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army. Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
