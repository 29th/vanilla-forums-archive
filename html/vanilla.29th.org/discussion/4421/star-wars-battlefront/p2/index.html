    I've been an alpha and beta tester for Origin since Battlefield 3....by filling out some forms...way back when...I forgot how I did it...it was a long time ago. As for the game. I have a report...

    Pros:
    Gameplay is definitely a lot of fun. Playing with my snow speeders and my imperial walkers have been kick ass and so much fun. The Star Wars Nostalgia is there. The music, the sound effects match everything and I do enjoy it. There's a lot of fun with vehicles. I find that to really be the bulk of the fun. The teams turned out to be incredibly balanced. The vehicles are very well balanced so there's no unfair advantage. I used to be an Imperial player all the time for the other battlefront games...but this game...Im digging the Rebels.

    The Cons:
    It's only in Alpha Stages. My task manager said the game was using over 2 million K of Memory. My teamspeak wont function because of that. Lots of computer lag trying to minimize it but the game does not lag at all for me.

    Final Analysis: I wouldn't buy another COD and Battlefield, Im iffy about but I'd buy this game.
