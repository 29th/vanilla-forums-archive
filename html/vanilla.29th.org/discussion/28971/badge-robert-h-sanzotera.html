Badge (Robert H. Sanzotera)

Technician, 4th Grade Adam K. Stachurski
October 2018 in Promotions and Awards

Sgt. Chinè said:

PFC Sanzotera started out in the 29th as someone who had a lot of problems, and not a lot of obvious solutions. showing up to the wrong servers, couldn't barely communicate , and was absolutely atrocious at shooting. When he got into my squad he was able to get a microphone, so i was able to help him more in depth and work with him more personally and throughout his time in Ap2S1, he has improved greatly. He can shoot better, communicate better, and he is even the clerk for AP2, and a good one at that. and since he became a PFC He has been working on his combat engineer AIT, and i am surprised to how easily he has picked it up. He has me so happlily suprised to his skill that i am happy to award him his Combat Engineer Marksman Badge, Congratulations PFC Sanzotera!
