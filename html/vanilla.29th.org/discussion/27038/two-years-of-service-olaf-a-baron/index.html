Two Years of Service (Olaf A. Baron)

T4 Ceglar [Ret.]
June 2018 in Promotions and Awards

Sgt. Runing said:

PFC Baron has been in the 29th for 2 years. He has served in many companies. I had the pleasure of having the PFC as an old squad mate in Baker, and I am happy to have him back in my squad. For his 2 years of serving I am happy to award him his 4th Army of Occupation Medal and his first World War I Victory Medal. Congratulations!
