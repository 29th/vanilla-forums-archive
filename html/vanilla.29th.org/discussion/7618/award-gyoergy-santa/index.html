Award (György Sánta)

PFC Ireson
November 2015 in Promotions and Awards

SSgt. Kruithof said:

During Platoon drills on Lutremange, PFC Santa was tasked to hold the North side of the North East village on Lutremange. He took position in the most North-Western bulding of the village. After some time, an enemy squad started to push in on the village from the North-Eastern and Eastern flank across a forest. PFC Santa switched his thompson to semi-automatic, and started to fire but only managed to supress the enemy. The first assault failed and the axis decided to flank towards the north of the village. PFC Santa spotted them and prepared himself for a new assault of the enemy. Under enemy fire he managed to pick every attacker off from his building. After that assault, the sole remaining enemy charged with a kubelwagen into the village. PFC Santa then moved out of his building to hunt down the remaining attacker with another rifleman. After some time, he found the enemy and took him out.

Overall, PFC Santa killed six enemies on his own, during the round, each time a few rounds of the thompson were heard and a kill message followed up. He managed to hold off an entire flank on his own against an enemy squad. His actions were crucial in the victory of S3, since most of the other members were defending the south of the village against a heavy assault from the other squad.
