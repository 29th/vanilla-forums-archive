Honorable Discharge (Noah A. Eckhardt)

T4 Ceglar [Ret.]
May 2018 in Discharges

HONORABLE DISCHARGE

FROM THE ARMED FORCES OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA

This is to certify that

Noah A. Eckhardt     PFC     EP1S2

was Honorably Discharged from the

UNITED STATES ARMY

on the 16th day of May 2018. This certificate is awarded

as a testimonial of Honest and Faithful Service





ARPC-OPM-O

DATE: 16th May 2018

MEMORANDUM FOR Commander,

U.S. Army 29th Infantry Division, ATTN: AHRC-OP-S, 1 Reserve Way

Fort Belvoir, VA 22060


SUBJECT: Unqualified Resignation


1.I hereby tender my unqualified resignation as an Enlisted Soldier in the

United States Army under the provisions of Chapter 6, Section III,

AR 135-175.

2.I understand that if my resignation is accepted I am entitled to an

honorable separation and will be furnished an Honorable Discharge

Certificate. I understand that I cannot resign if I am a statutory obligor.


Noah A. Eckhardt
Signature
Noah A. Eckhardt
Name (Type or Print)
Private, First Class Rifleman Easy Company, First Platoon, Second Squad
Rank Position Platoon/Squad

I just want to thank some people before I go away for a while. I know I was only here for about 4 months, but still.
Sgt. Andersson – I just want to say thank you for having me in your squad and being awesome. It was nice being in your squad joking around, occasionally doing some stupid stuff. I also really enjoyed your drills, even though I mostly died after the first few seconds/minutes and being PTUed as meatshield. I hope that S2 will stay the best squad in EP1 ;D
Sgt. van Dongen – Thank you for being an awesome DI and EL and helping me out if I had any questions about EL or ADI. Your squad got some cool guys in it (including you), try and keep it that way.
Sgt. Runing – I also thank you for being an awesome person. I especially thank you for your rants when the recruits came late to BCT. I hope your squad attendance will keep going up as it has the last few weeks.
PFC Lunden – Stop PTUing for meatshields.
SSgt. Bagge – I want to thank you for just being a good platoon leader overall. The drills were fun and good at the same time.
2Lt. Dashwood – I thank you for the same reason as SSgt., even though you came into EP1 later, it was quite fun participating in your drills even if I instantly died, like always.
PFC Baron – Thanks for helping me out at the beginning of being an EL and I am very sorry for bombarding you with questions, because I was too dumb to find it out myself.
T/5 Vonk – Thank you for being a good Lead Journalist and explaining the different things, although the CoC for (some) articles could be faster. Then again, I don’t know too much of how it works so maybe it is fast and I just don’t know it, because I don’t know how that stuff works, as mentioned above.
PFC Delgado – Thanks for being a cool SVE and explaining me how arma works. I also wanted to say sorry to you for being so slow in learning the arma basics.
PFC Rodgers – Thanks for accepting me into Adjutant Monitor, it was quite fun I have to say, sending out those PMs. And sorry for going out of bounds that one time.
Pvt. Olsen – Lastly, I want to thank you Pvt. Olsen (you should be PFC) for getting me into this whole thing! And for being a good friend. I wish you all good luck and success in the future! Ever forward!
