  • T5 Yukki
    September 2020

    On Saturday the 1st August, soldiers of DP1 had the first ever deployment in vietnam. SSgt. Novak leading his platoon with 14 chosen men won agains the 1st Cav in close quarter fighting on the city of Saigon.

    Attack round

    DP1 forces, consisting of 3 squads, started this round behind abandoned apartment block to the extreme east of the map with the task of capturing Cathedral building which was located in the middle of the map and was literally dominating the entire area around being surrounded with maze of huts to the North and West, rich urban quarters to the south and vast open avenue to the East. All 3 squads were instructed to approach and surround the building from all directions and launch a coordinated, simultaneous attack upon their mutual readiness. S1 lead by Sgt. Knezevic pushed its way through the streets around southern end of urban quarters, which were in close vicinity of the southern Cathedral walls, facing relatively little resistance from the enemies and stopped being hidden behind the half collapsed walls of the nearby gardens, having just a narrow road separating their position from objective. By that time, the joint forces of S2 and S3 were advancing directly to the North, crossing the easternmost end of the avenue leading to the objective and making their way to the nearby Park where S2, led by Sgt. Miochevich was tasked to hold the area and make it a base for their following dash to the objective which Eastern entrance was already within a hundred pace from their position. During the quick firefight, the Park area was successfully secured. S3, led by Cpl. Griffin, successfully bypassed the members of S2 and advanced through the Park to the West where many contacts with the enemy were established with their individuals pushing their way from the objecting towards the northeastern side of the map, literally counter attacking the Park area. Their efforts were in vain with all of them being quickly hunted and downed by combined efforts of S2 and S3. Upon dealing with the enemy sally, S3 successfully reached the designated position to the North and also West of the objective, making a swift progress through the hut area to the North of the cap. By that time, enemy forces were completely surrounded inside the objective and were demonstrating zero desire for an additional sally preferring to lock themselves inside the Cathedral and await DP1 final assault. Upon having all 3 squads in position, the order of simultaneous attack was given which resulted in attack from all possible directions with sections of S3 attacking from the hut’s area to the North and West, S2 attacking via the avenue to North and East and S1 making their progress from the South. Despite suffering some losses at the Eastern entrance of Cathedral where the enemy demonstrated relatively stiff resistance, DP1 made its way inside the Cathedral from all the attack directions and was involved in close combat with the enemy holding both floors of objective and desperately fighting for every inch of it. Losing roughly half its strength total, DP1 managed to secure the area killing all the enemies inside the cap.

    Defense round

    After successfully dealing with attack round, DP1 was now in the enemy shoes, having objective for the defense round. Sgt. Miochevich’s S2 was tasked with internal defense of the Cathedral and had all their members inside the cap. Sgt. Knezevic’s S1 was directed to the south of the objective where they were supposed to hold the entire southeastern area preventing the enemy from attacking through the urban quarters. Cpl. Griffin’s S3 disposition was Park area to the northeast of the objective with the idea of stopping the enemy to do what was actually done by S2 and S3 during round 1 – successfully capturing the entire Park and using it as a foothold for the further attack towards the cap zone. Right upon the round start, all 3 squads were quickly deployed according to previously mentioned plans and immediately got in contact with the enemy who was literally swarming the area in Cathedral vicinity. There was an intense gunfight for the S3 who faced enemies making their way to the Park and for S1 members were fighting off the enemies trying to push themselves through the South and East. Upon suffering heavy casualties but managing to put down just a few DP1 soldiers and having just a few members left, the remaining enemy started to play for picks lurking behind the buildings to the North and South, the tactic which, despite taking rather huge amount of time, led them to nowhere but eventual death and one more victory for DP1 which never gave ground during this round.
