Promotion (Jordan W. Zenhenko)

TSgt. Minor
November 2015 edited November 2015 in Promotions and Awards

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of


Jordan W. Zenhenko     166537141     BP3S2

I do promote him to Corporal in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 07th day of November 2015.

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.




2Lt. Perdiew said:

Today we have yet again carried out a time honored tradition in the 29th. A tradition where we examine an a candidate's competency to lead the men under his immediate command. This of course is known as the final step in a Squad Leader's Training program. In this scrimmage, PFC Zenhenko has been tested.

At the beginning of the scrim we told him he was going to be up against the full might of a massive armoured panzer division. This of course was a lie but nevertheless, PFC Zenhenko assessed the situation and made full use of what he had! With his rag tag group of infantry and his willy's jeeps, he gave this fearsome Axis force a run for their money! He displayed leadership in the face of adversity, which is what the 29th requests from all candidates in its SLT program!

PFC Zenhenko I say to you on the field lutremange of that you have achieved success in your final test of your SLT program. In the sight of all your brothers in arms, in the sight of the 29th I hear by deem thee worthy to enter the hallowed halls of leadership! You shall carry the flag of the 29th across the rocky shores of pointe de hoc! Through the concrete jungle of Berlin! And beyond the green savannahs of putot en Bessin!

PFC Zenhenko take a knee!

In the name of the soldier I charge you to be brave! In the name of the 29th I charge you to be steadfast! With this new chevron may you forever be an example to the men under your command! May your leadership make them as bullet proof as a tank, as fast as a willy's jeep, and as reliable as a as a revolver! Let it be known from this day forth that you shall officially be counted amongst those who bear the burden of leadership. And therefore I say to you PFC Zenhenko, ARISE! A CORPORAL OF THE 29th! Congratulations! Fix your name!
