Badge (Ross M. Dethfield)

Maj. Fritz
December 2019 in Promotions and Awards

Everytime I talk to TSgt. Dethfield, he talks about two things. Paperwork and Panzers. Even as a Germanist Eurocentrist academic, I can find it incredibly burdensome. Tank this, infantry support that, overwhelming firepower, etc. It goes on and on. With that said, absolutely no one can argue that there is anyone better that knows more about the application of mechanized weapons in the game of Squad. So, to show off his vast knowledge and skills, it is more than deserved to award TSgt. Dethfield his marksman armor badge. Congratulations TSgt.

Panzer Vor!
