Nine Years of Service (Kyle J. Wheatley)

CW4 Slomczynski
August 2016 in Promotions and Awards

1Lt. Fritz said:

I would like to say that Lieutenant Colonel Wheatley has been in the unit longer than I have been alive, but that would be a lie. In the past year, I would like to think that I have gotten to know the Lt. Col. pretty well. He has had a direct hand in making me the officer I am today and has done the same for many others. That is something that I can assure you takes patience and a vast amount of wisdom. In addition to that, he directly works with all the Company HQs, making sure we get what we need and that the 29th keeps on keeping on. Speaking of keeping on, he does that too, making sure the bills are paid and all that fun stuff. The rest is confidential. But in all seriousness, the Lt. Col. is a very active hand within the unit. He attends drills, hangs out in TS, and has helped bring this unit to where it is today which is no easy feat to maintain. So as always, thank you for your service and guidance over the years and hopefully one day you will make my first statement true (at least for someone). So congrats on your 18th Army of Occupation Medal and here is to 18 more!
