Ten Years of Service (Daniel A. Balzer)

T5 Kiostri [Ret.]
February 2019 in Promotions and Awards

@“Lt. Col. Wheatley” said:

There is a thing on Facebook known as the ten year challenge and ten years ago we all looked god awful and pimply. Some of you were just starting kindergarten. Meanwhile, FSgt. Balzer was still old as hell but he wasn't FSgt. Balzer he was little Pvt. Balzer. We had big plans for him even back then, some of them were higher than what he wanted. Ten years ago, ten times as long as my son has been alive, this little private joined the 29th and made his mark. It is therefore my distinct privilege to award him with his 20th AOCC and his 5th WW1 Victory Medal. Congratulations you old bastard, and here's to ten more.
