Award (Chris W. Robertson)

PFC Bae
August 2020 edited August 2020 in Promotions and Awards

2Lt. Nelson said:

Throughout our run of CBOTS, Sgt. Robertson gave his all with regards to planning and preparing his men to win. He and his squad thoughtfully analyzed the assortment of AO's and terrain types, looking at how best to take advantage of their various strong points, as well as how to avoid their weaknesses (many of the AO's had heavy terrain advantages for particular starting points, so there was a lot of intel to wrestle with). Sgt. Robertson did exactly that, picking his home-team AO's smartly, and planned how to counter the enemy squad, forcing them into a disadvantage. His key strategy was aggression - get into the objective with enough weight to ensure capture, and make the enemy push in to take them out. While not always successful, it did always result in heavy enemy casualties. With those defeats came opportunity to grow, and Sgt. Robertson made sure to utilize the ample footage provided by the video streams for review. He watched what the other squads did in their fights, and adapted his strategy to use what worked, and what didn't work. Due to his thorough preparation and execution, CP1S3 won the entirety of CBOTS with an admirable 26 points ahead of the 2nd place squad, CP2S2. Sgt. Robertson took his responsibilities as a Squad Leader to heart and pulled his sometimes-struggling squad together to do something legitimately impressive. That is all we can ask for in leadership! Congrats, Sarge, on earning the coveted Soldier's Medal. Ever forward!
