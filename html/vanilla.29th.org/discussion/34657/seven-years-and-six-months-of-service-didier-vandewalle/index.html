Seven Years and Six Months of Service (Didier Vandewalle)

T5 Vulpen
October 2019 in Promotions and Awards

PFC Tan said:

T/5 Vandewalle or “T/5 Vandy” is one of the most well known and familiar names not just in Charlie Company but all of the 29th ID. As a dedicated veteran, his performance in combat, his dedication and attitude has been nothing short of exemplary and outstanding. His stint in S1 so far has proved to me that he is a model soldier for all of his comrades around him to follow. For serving a total of an incredible 7 years and 6 months, I am pleased to award T/5 his 15th AoCC.
congratulations T/5!
