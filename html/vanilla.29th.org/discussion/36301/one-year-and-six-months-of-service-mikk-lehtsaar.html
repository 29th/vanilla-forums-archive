I am having the pleasure for knowing PFC Lehtsaar for years, back when he got transferred over to Able company couple of years ago. He left the unit two times, and he came back to the unit two times, both times when i asked him to. He did not object or make any excuses, and it's safe to assume i have deep respects to this guy.
