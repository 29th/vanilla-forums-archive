Seven Years Of Service (Daniel A. Balzer)

Cpt. Hill [Ret.]
January 2016 in Promotions and Awards

FSgt. Balzer is the marrow of Charlie company. His dedication is second to none and everything he does, he does with the goal of making each and every members' experience the best it can be. One of the longest serving members in the entire 29th with 7 years under his belt he continually strives to maintain Charlie and the 29th's standards.

Without him I would be lost and Charlie would not be the same. Thank you FSgt. for your years of service to the 29th.

*Salute*

14th Army Of Occupation Medal
7 years of uninterrupted service
