Badge (Alexander Boots) 

2Lt. Regan
March 2021 edited March 2021 in Promotions and Awards

SSgt. Newton said:

PFC Boots is one of the OG members of EP4S3 and is one of the faces that I am glad to see stick around from that time, as well as a couple of the other OG members. One of the things I noticed from Boots from day 1 in that squad was that he was an excellent player. Now at the time, Boots was a Pvt. and so he was knocking heads with his rifle. However, once he earned his PFC stripe he picked up the grenadier AIT and hasn't looked back. I have said in the past that PFC Boots often wins a lot of engagements that he has no business winning. This is because he is often one of the last players alive on his team, and somehow, someway, has an uncanny ability to win in that situation with nobody else on his team, or very few left on his team to help him. Often times when he pulls this off he is at a significant disadvantage in numbers. I cannot tell you how many times I have seen him save a round that otherwise would've been lost when he is fighting a 1v3 or a 1v4. This is in part because he is a patient man, and a bit crazy, but it is more so because he is an excellent grenadier. He is one of the best I have ever seen in the role at using both the optic rifle and the HE/Smoke launcher in tandem. So it is with great pleasure that I can award him his Grenadier Sharpshooter badge. Congratulations PFC Boots!
