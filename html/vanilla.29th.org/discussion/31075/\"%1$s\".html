Eight Years of Service (Carl J. Ternby) 

T5 Brown [Ret.]
April 2019 edited April 2019 in Promotions and Awards

Sgt. Christiansen said:

T/4 Ternby has been here for an inordinate amount of time, having dedicated 8 years to the 29th. He's spent much of that time mentoring and leading men of the 29th, and has certainly earned his place as one of our most senior veterans. More recently, he joined us here in Charlie, transferring to us back in february of 2018. In his time here, he's certainly proven himself as a capable soldier, and a wise member of Squad Two.

For 8 years of service, I am happy to award you your 16th Army of Occupation Medal, and your 4th World War 1 Victory Medal.
