Promotion and Badges (Jake Lee) 

PFC Noonan
March 2019 in Promotions and Awards

Cpl. Nguyen said:

Ever since Pvt. J. Lee joined, he has impressed not just me and PFC Tilley but also a great number of people in this platoon. He is a killer shot who consistently racks up kills every round with both the rifle and the submachine gun. Not only that, he always volunteers for leadership without hesitation and does an amazing job at it. Pvt. J. Lee also directly boosts the morale of the squad with funny videos, statistics of the squad since it was established, and great enthusiasm when inviting squad members to guest drills. Furthermore, he has proved himself as an enlistment liaison with only a few weeks in and helps me out tremendously in Light House. Pvt. J. Lee has been a shining example of what a Private should be. Thus, with great pride and pleasure, I would like to congratulate him on earning this promotion, and I look forward to even grander achievements from PFC J. Lee in the future.

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of

Jake Lee       961317618      DP2S2

I do promote him to Private, First Class in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 20th day of March 2019 .

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
