Promotion (Alex W. Crane)

T4 Ceglar [Ret.]
November 2019 in Promotions and Awards

2Lt. Kelso said:

I have known Cpl. Crane for quite some time. A disturbingly long amount of time once I thought about it. He is one of the most experienced members we have in the 29th, he has been involved in leadership in multiple different games and companies, and through all of this he still manages to be a excellent soldier on top of his leadership skills. He is known for his automatic rifleman skills in whatever game he touches, mowing down any poor fool that dares to take a shot at him. He took over my former squad and has turned S1 into a fiercer and more competitive group than I could of ever imagined. His continual hard work and dedication to the 29th has been noticed, in addition to work outside of drills, Cpl. Crane is available whenever needed to assist and frequently guests other drills to help. For his extremely hard work, and dedication to the 29th, I am proud to promote him to the rank of Sergeant.

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of

Alex W. Crane       004825138       EP3S1

I do promote him to Sergeant in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 3rd day of November 2019 .

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
