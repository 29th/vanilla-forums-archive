Three Years of Service (Patryk Borkowski)

Technician, 4th Grade Adam K. Stachurski
March 2018 in Promotions and Awards

SSgt. Novak said:

I still remember Pvt. Borkowski entering AP1S1 after BCT being shy, inexperienced and dependable. Time has passed, years have passed and his status has drastically changed. He not only managed to distinguish himself on Squad level with his Heavy skill and Platoon / Company level with his Clerk role but recently managed to move even further, getting his own squad. I did some research asking here and there how it actually paid off, and if it did at all, and, to my complete satisfaction, i never heard anything negative regarding this person's activity. Seems this last assignment made some good to all the parties involved and coincidentally he managed to hit his new service record milestone at the same time. Not much more to say but congrats him with his 6th Army of Occupation Medal!
