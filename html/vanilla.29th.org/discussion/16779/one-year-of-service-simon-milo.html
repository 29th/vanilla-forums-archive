One Year of Service (Simon Milo)

T4 Burg
February 2017 in Promotions and Awards

Sgt. Kear said:

I look back upon PFC Milo's first year in the 29th and he has already proven to be an indispensable asset; his work as a squad leader, keeping S2 afloat, is a glorious sight to behold and I am thankful for his continued service. Whilst the good PFC may have been thrust into leadership early he has proven himself time and time again that his skill and aptitude is beyond doubt. Therefore I am very pleased to award him his 2nd AOCC for One Year of Service and hope he sticks around for a few more!
