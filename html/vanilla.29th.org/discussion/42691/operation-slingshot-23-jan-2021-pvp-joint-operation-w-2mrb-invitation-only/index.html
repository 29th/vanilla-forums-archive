Operation Slingshot [23 Jan. 2021] (PvP Joint Operation w/ 2MRB) *Invitation only*

FSgt. Balzer [Ret.]
January 2021 edited January 2021 in 29th ID A3 Charlie Operations

29TH INFANTRY DIVISION
116TH REGIMENT, 1ST BN
OFFICE of the Lt. Col via
CHARLIE CO. HEADQUARTERS




WARNING ORDER (WARNO)

OPERATION SLINGSHOT
23 January 2021, 1600 EST

Situation
The security situation in Takistan has been deteriorating for several months. OPFOR separatists have gained ground, escalating attacks on BLUFOR bases supporting the central government. At the central government’s request, BLUFOR is launching Operation Slingshot, an offensive aimed at completing three key OPFOR objectives in Takistan.

Objectives:

  • Objective 1: Secure/Defend Training Camp [008,104].
  • Objective 2: Capture/Defend HVT [025,050].
  • Objective 3: Destroy/Defend Weapons Cache [108,063].

Map

  • Takistan [MAP OF AO TBD]

Mission

  • BLUFOR is tasked with completing three (3) OPFOR objectives.
  • OPFOR is tasked with defending three (3) objectives from BLUFOR.
  • Scenario Time Limit: 2 Hours.

Win Conditions

  • BLUFOR must secure all three objectives to win.
  • OPFOR must hold at least one objective to win.

Forces

  • BLUFOR
  • OPFOR (GRNFOR-type kit)

BLUFOR Equipment
Kits provided.

  • Any M4/M16 variant.
  • Any faction-correct 1x optic.
  • Any OCP vest/backpack as appropriate.
  • MARPAT optional if under 2MRB command.
  • Cosmetic customization only. DO NOT change kit composition.
  • Diffusal kits.

Assets:

  • 1x M1220 (O-GPK/M2) MRAP
  • 2x 1151A1 (GPK/M2) Humvee
  • 3x M1025A1 Humvee (unarmed)
  • 3x M1078A1P2 Transport Truck (unarmed)
  • MH-6M “Little Bird”

OPFOR Equipment:

  • Kits provided (slotting as RED, but using GRNFOR-type equipment).
  • Any AK47/AKM/N variant.
  • Any faction-correct 1x optic. Any GRNFOR vest/backpack as appropriate.
  • Cosmetic customization only. DO NOT change kit composition.
  • IEDs (# TBD)/Cell phones.

Assets:

  • Strongholds and Hideouts (see Scenario Parameters for more information)
  • 1x Offroad [Technical] (HMG) per stronghold
  • 1x UAZ 3151 (DshkM) per stronghold
  • 1x UAZ 3151 (SPG-9) per stronghold
  • Any civilian vehicle (unarmed)

Scenario Parameters

  • OPFOR Strongholds and Hideouts:
  • Strongholds: Main bases, motor pools.
  • Hideouts: Teleport poles close to objectives for moving infantry quickly.
  • Only OPFOR knows the locations.
  • Stronghold and hideout locations will be revealed to OPFOR command on the day of the event.
  • Unlimited respawns: Must be deployed in a group of at least four (4) soldiers.
  • Timed vehicle respawns: Armed vehicles take longer than unarmed vehicles to respawn.
  • Some members of the 29th may be assigned to 2MRB command in the interest of scenario balance.
  • Each side will have 1x Medic.

Rules of Engagement
BLUFOR

  • Free to fire on armed individuals, commander’s discretion.
  • PID targets
  • DO NOT fire on civilian vehicles without PID.
  • Avoid civilian casualties.
  • Civilian vehicles are off-limits.
  • Buildings may be entered to clear or garrison as needed.

OPFOR

  • No restrictions, except…
  • No vehicle-mounted IEDs.

Skills Focus

  • Asymmetrical warfare.
  • Operational coordination in a large AO.
