Promotion and Badge (Robert Friedrich)

T5 Valiūnas
November 2020 edited November 2020 in Promotions and Awards

Cpl. Elbayed said:

Pvt. Friedrich is a member of 1364nd Training Platoon. That Training Platoon is a very successful one. I am fortunate to have the two surviving members of such TP. Now back to Pvt. Friedrich. When I mention this name I remember how many time I died to the bullets of that Private’s rifle. I am happy that I am dying to him anymore but leading him and assigning tasks that he pulls out successfully. His Six months of service time which we celebrated very recently did give him the necessary material and experience to become a proper communicator and a more mature personality. Those times were not easy on him and I am glad that he stayed and pushed forward toward progress and now we see the result of such progress securing him his Expert Infantry Badge and his obviously promotion to the rank of Private, First Class. Congratulations PFC Friedrich. Keep doing what you are doing!

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of

Robert Friedrich       391667204      DP1S4

I do promote him to Private, First Class in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 26th day of November 2020 .

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
