Badge (Rens Jans-Beken)

T5 Lorne
July 2018 in Promotions and Awards

Sgt. Laird said:

Being a reliable and effective automatic rifleman is no easy feat. It isn't quite as simple as grab the biggest gun you can find out the arsenal and hold the trigger until it's time to reload; with the AIT comes a lot of strategic thinking which you have to do for yourself including finding good offensive and defensive positions and vary your fire-rate based on the squad's general mission. PFC Jans-Beken consistently displays all the abilities I would expect to find in an effective automatic rifleman, including the skill, work ethic, and perseverance. For this he is finally being awarded his Automatic Rifleman Marksman Arms Qualification Badge. Keep up the awesome work PFC, and congratulations!
