Promotion and Award (Christian D. Robles)

Maj. Dethfield
March 2016 in Promotions and Awards

Sgt. Lamb said:

T/5 Robles has been working exceptionally hard at his SLT and in his position as ASL of AP3S1. Whilst he had previously showcased his extreme diligence as Platoon Clerk for AP3 his performance over the last 6 months of SLT have been truly excellent. He has grown into his role as ASL over this time and Sgt. Littlefield has been most effusive with his praise for his right hand man. Time and time again T/5 Robles has taken the initiative in organizational matters, squad cohesion, battlefield leadership and integrating new members into the squad. He is an fantastic role model for all who enter into SLT and as such is being awarded his NCO Professional Development Ribbon. Furthermore all levels of leadership from squad to company HQ are of the opinion that a promotion to Corporal is here thoroughly well deserved. Congratulations Cpl. Robles!

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of

Christian D. Robles     109051248     AP3S1

I do promote him to Corporal in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 28th day of March 2016.

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
