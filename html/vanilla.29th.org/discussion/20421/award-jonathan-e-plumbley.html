Award (Jonathan E. Plumbley)

T5 Borkowski
September 2017 in Promotions and Awards

2Lt. Barclay said:

While PFC Plumbley did eliminate a lot of people with his rifle and mortar, his contribution to communications was critical to winning the last round. By finding a sweet spot outside of the Foxtrot objective wall, he was able to call over some allies and make a safe capture of the objective without having to go inside and clear out the warehouse. He repeated this while attacking the Gulf cap, finding a sweet spot outside a house wall and calling people over to help him capture.

PFC Plumbley has shown great skills of communication, leadership and ingenuity, securing the objectives and keeping friendly casualties to a minimum. Without his help in the critical last round there is no way to be sure that we would have won at all. PFC Plumbley's actions show the importance of scouting and thinking outside of the box, which is something that the whole unit can learn from.
For his action in the CAT match against AP2, it is my pleasure to award PFC Plumbley the Army Commendation Medal.
