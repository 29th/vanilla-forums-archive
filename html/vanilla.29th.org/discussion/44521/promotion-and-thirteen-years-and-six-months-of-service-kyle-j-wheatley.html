Promotion and Thirteen Years and Six Months of Service (Kyle J. Wheatley)

CSM Conrad
April 2021 edited April 2021 in Promotions and Awards

MSgt. Conrad said:

My partner in crime has finally achieved his dream position of a Regimental expansion. As such, I am proud to formally promote the leader of the 29th Infantry Division to the rank of Colonel, and finally award him his "full bird" like he's always wanted. You, the members of the 29th, have made this possible and we are grateful for your continued work. And also for the hard work of Colonel or something I guess. I promised him a quote, but I made sure he knew it wouldn't be very good. Additionally, Colonel Wheatley is being awarded his backlogged 25th, 26th, and 27th Army of Occupation Medals - an inclusion to this post that I will without a doubt get in trouble for.

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of

Kyle J. Wheatley       1197963558705       Regt. HQ

I do promote him to Colonel in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 8th day of April 2021 .

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
