    Closed Beta Begins - November 17th

    Rising Storm 2: Vietnam has officially reached Beta! We are looking to start the first phase of the (invite-only) Closed Beta on Thursday, November 17th. We will be selecting a small group of people from our mailing list, so make sure to be on the lookout for an email invitation on Thursday! (And check your Spam / Promotions folders!)

    What is the Closed Beta?

    Closed Beta for Rising Storm 2: Vietnam will have multiple phases that last only a few days (typically Thursday to Monday) and we will be inviting new players with each wave. If you don’t receive an invite now, hold tight as there will be multiple chances to get into the Closed Beta. Those who have been previously invited will be automatically eligible for future Beta phases.
