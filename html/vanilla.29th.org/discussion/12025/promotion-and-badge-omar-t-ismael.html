Promotion and Badge (Omar T. Ismael)

T5 Schelhaas [Ret.]
June 2016 edited June 2016 in Promotions and Awards

Cpl. Peroni said

Pvt. Ismael is one of the soldiers that, a month ago, were transferred to AP4S3. We always served in the same squad, and I can say that I know the guy pretty well. I had the chance to see him grow and mature during both of his tours in the unit, both as a 29ther and as a player, to the point were I can rely on him in every situation we may be presented with. I don't mean to exaggerate by complimenting him too much, but his skills make Pvt. Ismael one of deadliest guys in S3. On top of this, he is also showing to be a great assett from a staff positions angle - he is currently working on some projects with quartermasters you may see in the future, not to mention the forums theme we are using, which he single handedly designed. It is for all the reasons above that he is now being promoted to the rank of PFC. Congratulations, and use your new rank to further inspire those close to you, PFC.

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of


Omar T. Ismael     062653137     AP4S3

I do promote him to Private First Class in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the 31st day of MAY 2016.

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army. Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honor of the United States Army.
