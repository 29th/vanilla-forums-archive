Badge (Nikolas Reacher)

T4 Burg
April 2016 in Promotions and Awards

Cpl. Meyberg said:

Pvt. Reacher Has been with us for a little over 3 months. In these months Pvt. Reacher started out as just a Rifleman but along the way he also picked up the role of Combat life saver in squad two. This made it harder for him to to fully focus on the Rifle because now he also had to train in keeping people alive. Luckily for Squad two this was not a problem for Pvt. Reacher.

Due to the Hard work and training of Pvt. Reacher with the Rifle,
he as earned the Rifleman Marksman Badge.
