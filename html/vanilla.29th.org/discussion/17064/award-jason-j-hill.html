Award (Jason J. Hill)

CSM Conrad
March 2017 in Promotions and Awards

In the 29th, we greatly value our members that are always on the lookout for ways to improve the unit. Be it through dedication to their squads, helping out with a staff office project, or recruiting to expand the unit, we do our best to recognize all instances of these especially valuable members. A couple of weeks ago (and this is my fault for this being so late) we had one of these cases. Completely of his own initiative, CW3 discovered that Nuclear Fallout, the provider of our dedicated server, was running a special promotion that would allow us to lower the cost of our server by $10 a month. At his tip, Battalion HQ contacted NFO to take advantage of the promotion. Thanks to CW3 Hill, we were notified that our server cost was actually over-quote by $30 dollars a month. With one quick and easy message to the correct people, CW3 Hill has saved the unit a whopping $360 dollars in yearly server costs. In thanks for his model performance as a member of the 29th, CW3 Hill is being awarded a Good Conduct Medal.
