Award (Vincent Des Bois)

PFC Gillam [Ret.]
May 2015 in Promotions and Awards

SSgt. Bergstrom said:
During the 3 pronged SLT scrim of April 19, 2015 PFC Vincent Des Bois killed 10 men from two teams and secured victory for Cpl. Nelson. He demonstrated exceptional skill and accuracy with one of the shortest ranged weapons (TAR21) - iron sights only - defeated smoke screens and suppressing fire, flanked the enemy repeatedly and secured an entire flank on his own. All of this while carrying an AT weapon making all movement a costly stamina endeavor. He engaged targets at long range, short range, in controlled burst fire, supported automatic fire and accurate single shot.

I've played this game a lot and I've never in all my time seen anything like it. Without a doubt in my mind
PFC Des Bois has gone over, above, around and through the call of duty.
It is my pleasure and privilege to award PFC Des Bois the Silver Star.
