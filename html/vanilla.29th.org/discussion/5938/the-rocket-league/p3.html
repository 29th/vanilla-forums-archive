    ROCKET SUPERLIGA: 2nd season sign-ups

    It's happening! (Fellow cars, your patience moves me.) Let's try to start in late March or early April. All 29th members and friends are welcome. Teamspeak/PC/boost required. (Forum registration is public. They should've stopped me.)

    Want to participate? Please PM me or reply here.

    Players from the 1st season: Let us know what times/days of the week you're usually available. (Sorry to those who already replied.)

    New players, please include:
    - times and days when you can usually play
    - ping colour (green, yellow, orange, etc.)
    - player level (rookie, semi-pro, pro, veteran, etc.)
    - best ranked division (Challenger I, Bronze III, etc.)

    If you end up unable to commit, we're also building a substitutes list.

    (We're balancing teams based on ping and experience.)

    League format:
    - 3v3, Beckwith/Mannfield/DFH, normal weather
    - round-robin, playing each side at least 3 times, 1 point per win (goal difference is tiebreaker)

    Cup format:
    - 3v3, home advantage (bizarro custom settings determined by the higher seed)
    - knock-out tournament, seeding determined by league placing

    I'm thinking upwards of 7-8 matchdays (we can play several matches in an hour). Last time we had 4 teams. We can do 2v2 if our numbers are low, but this game's gotten popular, so hopefully we can get a big competition going.

    Once we get the ball rolling (ha ha) we can talk about forming teams, substitute lists, cross-season rewards, etc.
