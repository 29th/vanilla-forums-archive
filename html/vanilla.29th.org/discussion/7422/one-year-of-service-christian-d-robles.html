One Year of Service (Christian D. Robles)

T4 Lamb [Ret.]
October 2015 edited October 2015 in Promotions and Awards

Like any good stage show Ap3 needs both the actors and the backstage crew who keep everything smooth and ticking over. Well T5 Robles is our lights, sounds, prompter and props department. Not only that, he has been a member of the 29th ID as a whole for a full year, clerking (and PPSHing) away vigorously! Congratulations T5 on your 2nd AOCC!
