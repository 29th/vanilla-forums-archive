Promotion (Chad E. Stear)

Cpt. Hill [Ret.]
May 2016 edited May 2016 in Promotions and Awards

@2Lt. Bergstom said:

I've known Stear for a while now, he's worked with me in the Office jobs and did damn good work. Then he was an SL in Able before coming to us in Charlie. Now he's leading a squad for us and doing a pretty damn good job of it. Thus it's my pleasure to re-promote him to the rank of Corporal. Congrats Stear!

CERTIFICATION OF PROMOTION

TO ALL WHO SHALL SEE THESE PRESENTS, GREETING:

Know Ye, that reposing special trust and
confidence in the fidelity and abilities of

Chad E. Stear      986204957     CP1S2

I do promote him to    CORPORAL    in the

UNITED STATES ARMY

to rank as such from the    19th    day of    May 2016   .

You are charged to discharge carefully and diligently the duties of the grade to which promoted and to uphold the traditions and standards of the Army.

Effective with this promotion you are charged to execute diligently your special skills with a high degree of technical proficiency and to maintain standards of performance, moral courage and dedication to the Army which will serve as outstanding examples to your fellow soldiers. You are charged to observe and follow the orders and directions given by superiors acting according to the law, articles and rules governing the discipline of the Army. Your unfailing trust in superiors and loyalty to your peers will significantly contribute to the readiness and honour of the United States Army.
