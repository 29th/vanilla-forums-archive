Honorable Discharge (Ian Meyberg)

Cpt. Dashwood [Ret.]
August 2015 edited August 2015 in Discharges


HONORABLE DISCHARGE

FROM THE ARMED FORCES OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA

This is to certify that


Ian Meyberg    Corporal    Squad Leader

was Honorably Discharged from the

UNITED STATES ARMY

on the 04th day of August 2015. This certificate is awarded

as a testimonial of Honest and Faithful Service





ARPC-OPM-O

DATE: 04 AUG 2015

MEMORANDUM FOR Commander,

U.S. Army 29th Infantry Division, ATTN: AHRC-OP-S, 1 Reserve Way

Fort Belvoir, VA 22060


SUBJECT: Unqualified Resignation


1.I hereby tender my unqualified resignation as an Enlisted Soldier in the

United States Army under the provisions of Chapter 6, Section III,

AR 135-175.

2.I understand that if my resignation is accepted I am entitled to an

honorable separation and will be furnished an Honorable Discharge

Certificate. I understand that I cannot resign if I am a statutory obligor.


Ian Meyberg
Signature
IAN MEYBERG
Name (Type or Print)
Corporal Squad Leader Baker Company, Second Platoon, First Squad
Rank Position Platoon/Squad

From Cpl. Meyberg:

Hello All,
As all of you know I've not been able to join the drills I should've been joining in the last couple of months. This is due to my very busy life outside the 29th at the moment. I think his should not be an excuse to let down my responsibilities to the 29th but it did. I cannot combine all that's happening. So I have to make a decision on what is the best thing to do for myself and the squad. I feel I cannot effectively lead a squad at this point. So hereby I'm requesting an Honourable Discharge for myself. It's a hard thing to leave you guys but I feel it's the right decision. It has been an Honour serving with you all!

Cpl. Meyberg

Reason for leaving
